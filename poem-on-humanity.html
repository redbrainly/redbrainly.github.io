17. Read the poem “ caged bird” 1. What part of the poem strikes you the most and why? 2. What is the message of the poem about human rights/human freedom? 3. Write/compose a short poem about Human Rights (English/Filipino/Bicol):


Answer:

1. One part of the poem "Caged Bird" that strikes me the most is the line "The caged bird sings with a fearful trill of things unknown but longed for still." This line conveys the idea that despite being confined, the bird still has a desire for freedom and feels a longing for something beyond its current circumstances.

2. The message of the poem about human rights/human freedom is about the struggle for equality and justice. The caged bird symbolizes those who are oppressed and denied their basic rights and freedoms, while the free bird represents those who have the privilege and ability to live freely. The poem highlights the disparity between the two and the need for equal treatment and rights for all.

23. simple poem about human rights.​


“Poem About My Rights” By: (June Millicent Jordan)

June Jordan (1936-2002) was a bisexual Jamaican-American poet, teacher, essayist, and activist. Issues like gender, immigration, and race frequently came up in her writing. She also emphasis the importance of intersectionality when it came to struggles for equality and human rights. In “Poem About My Rights,” Jordan centers on race, rape, and gender inequality. Jordan details all the things that are “wrong” about her – wrong color, wrong sex, wrong continent. The poem is vivid and visceral, coming to its peak in the section:

I am the history of rape

I am the history of the rejection of who I am

I am the history of the terrorized incarceration of

Myself

I am not wrong: Wrong is not my name

My name is my own my own my own

In a sweet, illumining and fulfilling way.

Humility

Is not the helpless surrender

Humility

Is not a frightened child.

Humility

Is real receptivity in us.

If we receive with devoted humility,

Humility

Is the secret of secrets

For self-expansion,

For world-inspiration

For world-transformation.

You are really great.

Your humility

Has erased earth-anger.

You are really good.

Your humility

Has fed Heaven-hunger

Humility is not humiliation.

No, never!

Humility is an inner light

That genuinely inspires

The rest of the divine virtues

To come to the fore

And illumine the world.

Humility

Soulfully follows

Happiness.

Humility

Is

An extraordinary achievement.

Humility is the capacity

That expands.

Humility is the capacity

That dives deep within.

Humility is the capacity

Humility can be made very vast.

Humility can give us

Inseparable oneness

With God’s Delight.

The difference

Humility is manifested

We should become

Humility-gratitude-flood.

Humility covers

The length and breadth of the world
