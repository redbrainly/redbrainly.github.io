1/2mv^2 = 200 J

30. Calculate the potential energy of a 5 kg object sitting on a 3m high ledge.


Potential Energy

[tex]______________________________________[/tex]

Calculate the potential energy of a 5 kg object sitting on a 3m high ledge.

Given:

Mass - 5 kgHeight - 3 m

Answer & Solution:

PE = mghPE = (5 kg)(9.8 m/s²)(3 m)PE = (49 kg.m/s²)(3 m)PE = 147 kg.m²/s²PE = 147 J

Hence, the potential energy of an object is 147 J.
