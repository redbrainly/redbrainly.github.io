30. Matt is five years older than his brother Mike. In 4 years, the sum of their ages 39 years. Find the present age of mike. Please put a solution.


Present Ages of Matt and Mike:

Matt is 18 years old.

Mike is 13 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Age Word Problem

The problem is asking for the present ages of Matt and Mike. To find the answer, you have to analyze the given facts in the problem and identify the known and unknown.

Given:

Matt - five years older than his brother Mike

39 - the sum of their ages in 4 years

Present Ages:

Let x be Mike's present age.

Then, x + 5 be Matt's age.

In 4 Years:

Mike - x + 4

Matt - x + 5 + 4

Equation:

(x + 4) + (x + 5 + 4) = 39

Solution:

Mike's Present Age:

Combine similar terms.

(x + 4) + (x + 5 + 4) = 39

x + 4 + x + 5 + 4 = 39

2x + 13 = 39

2x = 39 - 13

2x = 26

2x/2 = 26/2

x = 13

Mike's present age is 13 years old.

Matt's Present Age:

Plug in Mike's age.

x + 5 = 13 + 5 = 18

Matt's present age is 18 years old.

Final Answer:

Mike's present age is 13 years old.

Matt's present age is 18 years old.

Checking:

(x + 4) + (x + 5 + 4) = 39

(13 + 4) + (18 + 4) = 39

17 + 22 = 39

39 = 39 ✔
