16. examples of samar proverbs


The Samar Proverbs are also known as the Waray qoutes in which sayings/qoutes are translated into the dialect of Samar (Waray). These proverbs or qoutes are used as reminders in life by most of the people up until these days.

Examples of Samar Proverbs:

1. "Ang gintikangan buhaton tapusa anay, san-o magtikang hin iba nga buruhaton."

(Finish what you have started before starting anew.)

2. "Kun ano an gintanom, amo man it aanihon."

(You shall harvest what you have planted.)

3. "Kun ano an puno, amo man an bunga."

(What has been the tree, must bear its kind of fruit.)

28. Island of samar history


Answer:

On June 19, 1965, through Republic Act No. 4221, Samar was divided into three provinces: Northern Samar, Western Samar and Eastern Samar. The capitals of the provinces are, respectively, Catarman, Catbalogan City, and Borongan City.[4] In commemoration of the establishment of these provinces, June 19 is celebrated as an annual holiday and many have the day off from work.
