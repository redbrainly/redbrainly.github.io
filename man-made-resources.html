24. Give the definitions of the following terms: 1. Natural resources 2. Man-made resources 3. Renewable energy resources 4. Non- renewable energy 5. Biodiversity 6. Watershed 7. Minerals


1. Natural Resources :materials or substances such as minerals, forests, water, and fertile land that occur in nature and can be used for economic gain.

2. Man-made Resources: The definition of man maderefers to something that was created by humans, as opposed to by God or nature.

3. Renewable energy resources: Renewable energy is energy that is collected from renewable resources, which are naturally replenished on a human timescale, such as sunlight, wind, rain, tides, waves, and geothermal heat.

4. Non-renewable energy: A non-renewable resource is a resource of economic value that cannot be readily replaced by natural means at a quick enough pace to keep up with consumption.

5. Biodiversity:  Biodiversity refers to the variety and variability of life on Earth. 

6. Watershed: an area or ridge of land that separates waters flowing to different rivers, basins, or seas.

7. Minerals: Minerals are substances that are formed naturally in the Earth. 

Natural resources are earth’s resources itself. Man-made resources as stated, is made by human beings artificially. Renewable resources are common and can be replaced. Non renewable resources are not replaced easily. Soil can be replaced in a thousand years. Biodiversity is the living life thriving in an environment. Minerals are earth underground resources.
