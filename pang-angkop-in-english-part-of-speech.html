21. List down the 8 Parts of Speech in your English notebook. Write the definition each part and give 5 examples each​


Answer:

Noun

It names a person, place, or thing

doctor, teacher, dog, cat, house

Verb

It describes an action

realize, look, sit, walk, run

Preposition

It is used before a noun/pronoun or direction in a sentence

about, for, from, in, at

Pronoun

It replaces the name of a person, place, or thing

she, his, it, their, that

Adverb

It tells how often, how, when, and where or describes a verb, adjective, or adverb

loudly, always, never, soon, late

Adjective

It describes a noun or pronoun

tall, short, blue, yellow, wide

Conjunction

They join words or group of words in a sentence

and, because, therefore, yet, since

Interjection

They express strong emotion

Wow! Bravo! Yeah! Oh my God! Yay!
