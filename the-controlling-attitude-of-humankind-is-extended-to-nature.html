Theories to Show Care About the Environment

1. Deep Ecology

– For this theory, ecological crisis is a part of anthropocentrism. The controlling attitude of humankind is extended to nature, when in fact, humanity is part of nature.

2. Social Ecology

– For this theory, ecological crisis results from authoritarian social structures.

– Destroying nature is a reflection wherein few people overpower others while exploiting the environment for profit and self-interest.

3. Eco-feminism

– This theory argues that ecological crisis is a consequence of male dominance.

– Whatever is “superior” is entitled to whatever is inferior.

– Freeing nature means removing what is superior and inferior.

– This theory values care, conservation, preservation of nature and humanity.
