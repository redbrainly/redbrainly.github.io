1. 2. What is the difference between bust to bust, upper bust, and lower bust bodymeasurement?​


Answer:

Measurement will end up with a Bodice Block that has an Upper Bust measurement 2 inches smaller than the Bust Measurement. This is fine if you are a B-bust cup, but all other bust cups will end up with the wrong amount of ease in the underarm (Upper Bust) area.

9. paano na a measure ang upper bust​


Step 1. Grab a friend, a measuring tape, a long piece of thin elastic, and a pen and paper.

Step 2. Wear the underwear that you would normally wear under the dress.

Step 3. Tie the elastic around the smallest part of your torso, which is your waist.

Step 4. Stand up straight and tall. Stay relaxed. Try not to hold your breath, suck in or push out your stomach or chest. Take all the measurements with your arms by your sides

Step 5. Begin taking the required measurements. Take them more than once to be sure.

The upper bust is measured at the most protruding part of your shoulder blades, above the fullest part of your bust. Make sure to measure horizontally straight, so that the tape grazes through your armpit.
