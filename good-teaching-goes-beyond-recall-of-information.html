5. Why does authentic freedom goes beyond being free from constraints or limitations?


Authentic freedom goes beyond being free from constraints or limitations because true freedom involves more than just the absence of external constraints. It also involves being able to make choices and decisions that align with your own values, goals, and interests. In other words, true freedom is not just about being able to do whatever you want, but about being able to act in accordance with your own sense of agency and autonomy.
