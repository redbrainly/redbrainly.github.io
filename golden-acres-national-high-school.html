10. The investor group bought 8 2/3 acres of land for $182,000 what was the cost of each acre


Answer:

$21,000

Step-by-step explanation:

Since 8 2/3 isn't a whole number, you need to make it an improper fraction, which is 26/3 and then, you need to divide 182,000 by 26 which is 7,000 so 1/3 is 7,000 . You need to multiply 7,000 by 3 to get the cost of 1 acre.
