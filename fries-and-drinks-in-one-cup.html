10. a restaurants menu includes pancakes, waffles, and home fries For drinks they have coffee,hot chocolate,and tea .how many different choices of food and drinks can a customer have?​


Answer:

9 choices

Step-by-step explanation:

In this kind of question, usually we deal with it using the common rule. It says if there are m choices for the first event, and n choices for the second event given the first even, then we simply multiply these two values. That is,

[tex]a = mn[/tex]

We denote the number of choices as a.

Solve.

[tex]a = mn \\ a = (3)(3) \\ a = 9[/tex]

Therefore, there are 12 different choices a costumer can choose from.

ALTERNATIVE EXPLANATION

We could assign variables to the items on the menu like the following:

[tex]x= pancakes \\ y = waffles \\ z= home \: fries \\ a = coffee \\ b = hot \: chocolate \\ c = tea[/tex]

We could also divide them into two sets, namely set F and set D.

F={x, y, z}

D={a, b, c}

Now we find their cartesian product. We have,

F x D= {xa, xb, xc, ya, yb, yc, za, zb, zc}

This method also gives us 9 choices or 9 different ways.

20. The cost of the cup of coffee, a sandwich, and fries is p49. the sandwich costs twice as much as the coffee. the fries costs p17 more than the coffee. what is the cost of the coffee?


Answer:

The cost of the coffee is ₱8.

Explanation:

Let:

x + 17 = cost of the fries

2x = cost of the sandwich

x = cost of the coffee

x + 17 + 2x + x = 49

4x + 17 = 49

4x = 49 - 17

4x = 32

x = ₱8 <= cost of the coffee

29. At a restaurant four people order fried crab claws and four people order a cup of gumbo, with a total bill of $31. If only two people had ordered the crab claws and one person ordered the gumbo, the bill would have been $12.25. How much is each order of fried crab claws and each cup of gumbo?


Answer:

Each crab claws is = $4.5Each cup of gumbo = $3.25

Step-by-step explanation:

Information:Four people order fried crab claws and four people order a cup of gumbo, with a total bill of $31.If only two people had ordered the crab claws and one person ordered the gumbo, the bill would have been $12.25. Solution:

Let,

The cost of a crab claw be = x

The cost of a gumbo be = y

Now, in first case,

Four people ordered crab claws = 4x

Four people ordered a cup of gumbo = 4y

Their total bill was = $31

So, the equation thus formed here is

→ 4x + 4y = 31        ___ (I)

Then, in second case,

Two people ordered crab claws = 2x

One people ordered a gumbo = y

Their total bill was = $12.25

So, the equation thus formed here is

→ 2x + y = 12.25

or y = 12.25 - 2x            ___ (II)

Now, by substitution method,

Substituting eqn. (II) in eqn. (I) we get,

⇒ 4x + 4(12.25 - 2x) = 31

[On opening the brackets]

⇒ 4x + 49 - 8x = 31

[Transposing like terms to either side]

⇒ 8x - 4x = 49 - 31

[On simplifying]

⇒ 4x = 18

[On further simplification]

⇒ x = 4.5

So now,

Putting x = 4.5 in eqn. (II) we get,

→ y = 12.25 - 2(4.5)

→ y = 12.25 - 9

→ y = 3.25

Conclusion:

Hence,

The required cost of each crab claws is $4.5, and each cup of gumbo is $3.25 (Ans)
