4. consumer act salient points


Answer:

Primary Focus is given on consumer right's protection

Explanation:

The Philippine Consumer Act or RA 7394 provides for the following rights and basic tenets:

protection against hazards to health and safety; protection against deceptive, unfair and unconscionable sales acts and practices; provision of information and education to facilitate sound choice and the proper exercise of rights by the consumer; provision of adequate rights and means of redress; and involvement of consumer representatives in the formulation of social and economic policies.

All of these point to the rights of a regular consumer being protected. We can most relate with is under #2. Oftentimes, when we encounter deceptive sales practices, we just let it go instead of complaining about it. But there is actually a law that protects Filipinos.

The consumer acts also provides against the "no return no exchange policy". Buyers can always return products that are defective in exchange for another product of the same value or if not, a full refund. It does not usually apply, however, if you simply changed your mind about the purchase.
