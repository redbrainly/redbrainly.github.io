1. How important is the electrical plan in terms of load schedule​.


Answer:

Preparing a load schedule helps in determining the amount of power required for an installation. The load schedule helps electrical engineers to properly size conductors, conduits and protection systems. In addition, it helps designers to properly size the power backup generators or other alternative power sources.

2. how important is the electrical plans in terms of load schedule?​


Answer:

Preparing a load schedule helps in determining the amount of power required for an installation. This information is then used by electrical engineers to properly size conductors, conduits and determine the proper overload and other protection and control systems.
