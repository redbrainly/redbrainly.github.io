ADVANTAGES

Advantages of the thresher include less physical labour and more efficiency (amount of grain thresher per amount of time). Less seed breakage is also a benefit of using a thresher as opposed to stomping or beating grains. However, more breakage can occur it is not used properly.

Answer:

1. Four wheel tractor - A 4 wheel drive tractor has power in all 4 wheels to improve traction, increase pulling power, and enhance versatility. It helps rear wheels to pull tractor forward and thus the rear wheels have better traction and reduced slippage.

2. Grass cutter is a machine utilizing one or more revolving blade to cut a grass surface to an even height.

3. Rice Seeder - It is a machine designed for sowing germinated paddy seed directly in wetland field.

4. Corn Seeder is mainly used for corn sowing
