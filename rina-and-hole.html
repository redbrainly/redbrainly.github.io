28. Rina's father is 3 years more than twice as old as Rina. The product of their ages in years is 560. How would you represent Rina's father age?​


Answer:

Rina's age is 16 while her father's age is 35

Rina's father age is represented by x = 3 + 2y

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x = age of Rina's father

y = age of Rina

Given:

x = 3 + 2y Eq. 1

(x)(y) = 560 Eq. 2

Substitute Eq. 1 to Eq. 2

(3 + 2y)(y) = 560

3y + 2y² = 560

2y² + 3y = 560

2y² + 3y - 560 = 0

By quadratic formula

ROOTS: -35/2 and 16

xy = 560

x = 560/y

x = 560/ 16

x = 35
