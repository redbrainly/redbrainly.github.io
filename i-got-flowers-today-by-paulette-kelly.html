Answer:

Kelly saved $37.

Step-by-step explanation:

Indhu's savings: x

Kelly's savings: x + 16  (16 more than Indhu's)

Marvin's savings: 2x  (twice the amount Indhu saved)

Equation:

x + (x+16) + (2x) = 100

4x + 16 = 100

4x = 100-16

Solve for Indhu's savings, x:

4x/4 = 84/4

x = 21

Hindhu saved $21.

Find how much Marvin and Kelly saved:

Kelly: x+16  ⇒ 21+16=  $37

Marvin: 2x  ⇒  2(21) = $42

Kelly saved $37.

Check:

$21 + $37 + $42 = $100

$100 = $100 (True)

24. Mrs. Kelly creates a garden. She buys 15 flowers and 3 bushes for a total cost of $160.32. Each bush costs $11.50 more than each flower. How much does each bush cost? Group of answer choices


Answer:

Each bush cost $18.49.

Step-by-step explanation:

Determine what are the given numbers.15 flowers, 3 bushes, total cost $160.32, each bush $11.50 more than each flower

2. Representation:

Let x be the cost of each flowerLet y be the cost of each bush

y= x + $11.50

3. Solve.

[tex]15x + 3y = 160.32 \\ 15x + 3(x + 11.50) = 160.32 \\ 15x + 3x + 34.50 = 160.32 \\ 15x + 3x = 160.32 - 34.50 \\ 18x = 125.82 \\ \frac{18x}{18} = \frac{125.82}{18} \\ x = 6.99[/tex]So each flower cost $6.99. Since the cost of the bush is $11.50 more than each flower, just add the cost of the flower with $11.50.[tex]y = x + 11.50 \\ y = 6.99 + 11.50 \\ y = 18.49[/tex]To ensure that the answer is correct, let's do the checking.[tex]15x + 3y = 160.32 \\ 15(6.99) + 3(18.49) = 160.32 \\ 104.85 + 55.47 = 160.32 \\ 160.32 = 160.32[/tex]We come up with equal sum, so our answer is correct.
