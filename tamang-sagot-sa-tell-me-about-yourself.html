30. tell me about yourself​


Tell me about yourself is a common question during an interview. It gives the interviewee an overview of the person. The secret formula to give the best answer is to tell about your present and past job experience respectively. Mention your current job role or recent accomplishment. In terms of previous experience, mention a specific skill that you learn.

How to answer “Tell me about yourself”

These are the best way to answer the question “Tell me about yourself:”

Mention previous experiences and proven successes relevant e to the position.  Determine how your current job relates to the applied job. Focus on strengths and abilities that you can claim with examples.  Highlight your personality when answering.

Good Words to Describe Yourself

These are some good words to describe yourself:
