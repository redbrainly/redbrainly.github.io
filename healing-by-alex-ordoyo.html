Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x = Alex's mass.

Let y = my mass.

Eq. 1 : 4x/7 = 2y/5

Eq. 2 : y = 18 + x

Eq. 1 * 35 : 20x = 14y (Eq. 3)

Eq. 2 * 14 : 14x + 252 = 14y (Eq. 4)

Eq. 3 - Eq. 4 : 6x - 252 = 0

Alex's mass: 252 ÷ 6 = 42 kg.
