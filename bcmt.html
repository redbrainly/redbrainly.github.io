18. the most active volcano in the philippines isA.TaalB.Mt. MayonC.Mt. KanlaonD.Mt. Iraya​


Answer:

Most Active Volcanoes in the Philippines

The most active volcano in the Philippines is Mount Mayon, with over 50 to 60 historical eruptions. The correct answer is letter B.

Explanation:

In the Philippines, there are more than 300 volcanoes, and 24 of them are categorized as active volcano. According to PHIVOLCS, to categorize a volcano as active, they must satisfy one of these two conditions: First, it should have erupted within the last 600 years with accompanying records or testaments from witnesses; Second: in the absence of any historical eruptions, the geological deposits obtained should yield an age of 10,000 years or younger.

Out of all the active volcanoes in the country, Mount Mayon ranks first, followed by Taal Volcano (2nd most active), Kanlaon Volcano (3rd most active), and Bulusan Volcano (4th most active).
