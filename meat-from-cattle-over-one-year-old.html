25. Meat of cattle over 1 year old:a. beef b. carabeef c. pork d. chevon


Answer:

Meat of cattle over one year old is: a. beef

Explanation:

Carabeef- is a combined word from carabao and beef. Therefore, it means meat of the water buffalo or carabao.  

Beef- is a meat of cattle. This is classified by grade from its degree of maturity and intramuscular fat.

Pork- is a meat of pig. Pork is classified according to which part of the pig it came from.  

Chevon- is a lean tender red meat of a goat. Also, take note that goat meat can also be known as cabrito, capretto or kid depending on the age of the goat.  Meat from adult goats is referred to as chevon and cabrito, while from young goats, it is capretto, natale or kid.

Basing it from the definition itself, beef is the answer because it came from a cattle. Without considering the maturity, the other choices would be automatically crossed out for they came from different animals.
