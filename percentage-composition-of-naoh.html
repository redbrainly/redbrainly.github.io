4. If 25 grams of NaOH is dissolved in 175 grams of water, the percentage solute is equal to?


✏️PERCENT BY MASS

[tex]\tt{\huge{\green{Solution:}}}[/tex]

The problem asks to calculate for the percent by mass of solute. First, we need to identify the given values. These are

(Note: NaOH is the solute and water is the solvent)

mass of solute = 25 g

mass of solvent = 175 g

Finally, we can now calculate the percent by mass of the solute. Therefore, the percent by mass is

[tex]\text{percent by mass} = \dfrac{\text{mass of solute}}{\text{mass of solute + mass of solvent}} \times 100\%[/tex]

[tex]\text{percent by mass} = \dfrac{\text{25 g}}{\text{25 g + 175 g}} \times 100\%[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\text{percent by mass} = 12.5\%}[/tex]

17. 1. How many moles of NaOH are present in 45.0g NaOH?​


PROBLEM

How many moles of sodium hydroxide (NaOH) are present in 45.0 g NaOH?

ANSWER: There are 1.125 moles.

SOLUTION:

» Find the atomic mass of the constitutent elements, and the molar mass of the compound.

mass of sodium (Na) = 23 g/molmass of oxygen (O) = 16 g/molmass of hydrogen (H) = 1 g/mol

The molar mass of a substance is the sum of the mass of the atoms present in the chemical formula of the said compound. Thus, the formula mass for NaOH is

[tex] \quad \Rightarrow \rm{ (23 \:g/mol ) + (16 \:g/mol) + (1 \:g/mol)} [/tex]

[tex]\quad \large \Rightarrow \rm 40\:\:g/mol [/tex]

» Find the number of moles

[tex] \text{no. of moles} = \frac{\text{mass of substance in grams}}{\text{molar mass of substance}} [/tex]

[tex] \text{no. of moles} = \rm \frac{ 45.0\:g }{40.0\:g\cdot mol^{-1}} [/tex]

[tex] \text{no. of moles} = \rm \frac{ 45.0\:\cancel{g} }{40\:\cancel{g}\cdot mol^{-1}} [/tex]

[tex] \large \green{ \text{no. of moles} = \rm 1.125\:mol} [/tex]

Therefore, 1.125 moles of NaOH are present in 45.0g NaOH.

18. What is the percentage by mass of 106.5 g NaOH solute in 556.5 g solution?


SOLUTION:

Step 1: List the given values.

[tex]\begin{aligned} & \text{mass of solute} = \text{106.5 g} \\ & \text{mass of solution} = \text{556.5 g} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Step 2: Solve for the percent by mass using the formula.

[tex]\begin{aligned} \text{percent by mass} & = \frac{\text{mass of solute}}{\text{mass of solution}} \times 100\% \\ & = \frac{\text{106.5 g}}{\text{556.5 g}} \times 100\% \\ & = \boxed{19.14\%} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Hence, the percent by mass of NaOH in the solution is 19.14%.

24. A. Percentage Composition Refer to the examples below to compute for the percentage composition of the following: 1) Lye (NaOH) 2) Rust (Fe2O3) 3) Boric Acid (H3BO3) 4) Simple Sugar (C6H12O6) 5) Calamine (Zn2SiO4 H₂O)​


Answer:

Calculations for the percentage composition of each compound:

:Lye (NaOH):

Molecular weight of NaOH = 23 g/mol

Mass of Na = 23 x 1 = 23 g

Mass of O = 16 x 1 = 16 g

Mass of H = 1 x 1 = 1 g

Molecular weight = 23 + 16 + 1 = 40 g/mol

% of Na = (23 / 40) x 100 = 57.5%

% of O = (16 / 40) x 100 = 40%

% of H = (1 / 40) x 100 = 2.5%

Rust (Fe2O3):

Molecular weight of Fe2O3 = 159 g/mol

Mass of Fe = 55.8 x 2 = 111.6 g

Mass of O = 16 x 3 = 48 g

Molecular weight = 111.6 + 48 = 159.6 g/mol

% of Fe = (111.6 / 159.6) x 100 = 70%

% of O = (48 / 159.6) x 100 = 30%

Boric Acid (H3BO3):

Molecular weight of H3BO3 = 61 g/mol

Mass of B = 10.8 x 1 = 10.8 g

Mass of O = 16 x 3 = 48 g

Mass of H = 1 x 3 = 3 g

Molecular weight = 10.8 + 48 + 3 = 61 g/mol

% of B = (10.8 / 61) x 100 = 17.7%

% of O = (48 / 61) x 100 = 78.7%

% of H = (3 / 61) x 100 = 4.9%

Simple Sugar (C6H12O6):

Molecular weight of C6H12O6 = 180 g/mol

Mass of C = 12 x 6 = 72 g

Mass of H = 1 x 12 = 12 g

Mass of O = 16 x 6 = 96 g

Molecular weight = 72 + 12 + 96 = 180 g/mol

% of C = (72 / 180) x 100 = 40%

% of H = (12 / 180) x 100 = 6.7%

% of O = (96 / 180) x 100 = 53.3%

Calamine (Zn2SiO4 H2O):

Molecular weight of Zn2SiO4 H2O = 247 g/mol

Mass of Zn = 65.4 x 2 = 130.8 g

Mass of Si = 28 x 1 = 28 g

Mass of O = 16 x 4 = 64 g

Mass of H = 1 x 2 = 2 g

Molecular weight = 130.8 + 28 + 64 + 2 = 247 g/mol

% of Zn = (130.8 / 247) x 100 = 52.9%

% of Si = (28 / 247) x 100 = 11.3%

% of O = (64 / 247) x 100 = 25.9%

% of H = (2 / 247) x 100 = 0.8%
