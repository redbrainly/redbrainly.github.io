29. How the RPRH Law can curb the cases of teenage pregnancy?​


How the RPRH Law can curb the cases of teenage pregnancy?​

Just by its design and purpose, we can tell that the RPRH Law will have a certain impact when it comes to lessening the cases of teenage pregnancy. It will, however, go against the tide of modernization that comes with heightened sexual awareness among teens.  

Here are some of the things the law is pushing:  

Hospitals are required to provide family planning methods, both contraceptives, and natural, although religious health facilities are exempted out of respect for their rights. A formal Sex Education will be taught in schools to provide a counterbalance against what they may learn from the street. It touches on topics such as the formation of values, the rights of women an children, population and development, family planning, creating a responsible relationship, and sexually transmitted diseases. The government also required the availability of health facilities complete with proper information even in the most rural areas.  The information and health providers have another incentive to take all these things very seriously since the government promises to prosecute those who neglect their duties.

I do not expect that this to work 100%, but it will help people especially the teens in terms of making the right decisions when the time comes.
