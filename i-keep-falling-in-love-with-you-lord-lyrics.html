6. Exodus 15:2626 and said, “If you diligently heed the voice of the Lord your God and do what is right in His sight, give ear to His commandments and keep all His statutes, I will put none of the diseases on you which I have brought on the Egyptians. For I am the Lord who heals you.”​


Answer:

Diseases mark the effects of the divine curse. God has the power and the mercy to heal (Deut. 32:39; Ps. 103:3).

He alone can heal in the peace of His salvation (2 Chr. 16:12; Is. 38:17).

In this particular example the Lord was promising to heal Israel of all their diseases. This is not a promise for all people at all times in history. Health and wealth preachers who guarantee present healing are right in an ultimate sense that all physical diseases are healed in Christ, but they are putting the future realities into present situations in a way that Scripture does not.

The kingdom is here,but we are waiting for its full reality. As a result, he is causing legitimately righteous believers to constantly accuse themselves. There is nothing in Scripture that teaches that healing can no longer happen today. God is still active in doing miraculous things. However, we have to observe that the time of the apostles we a unique period where healing was a more common event. It is more rare in our time. However, this does not mean that it has completely ceased. We can pray for healing along with seeking medical help.

The Lord will choose to answer our prayers in the way that He sees fit. For particular examples of righteous believers struggling we have Job in the Old Testament and Timothy in the New Testament.
