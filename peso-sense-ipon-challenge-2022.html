14. a man bought a house at the beginning of 2010 for 750000 pesos.if it increases in value at 7500 pesos each year, how much was it worth at the end of 2022?


Answer:

P840 000

Step-by-step explanation:

A man bought a house at 2010 for 750 000 pesos. If it increases at 7500 pesos each year, how much is it worth at the end of 2022?

2022 - 2010 = 12

It will be 12 years by then.

12 x 7500 = 90 000

In 12 years, it will increase by 90 000 pesos.

750 000 + 90 000 = 840 000

In 2022, the value of the house is P840 000.

29. 1ano ibig sabihin ng konseptong mag ipon 2. bakit mahalaga mag ipon


IBIG SABIHIN NG KONSEPTONG MAG IPON

Answer:

Ang bagong taon ay madalas na nagbibigay ng hamon para sa bagong pokus. Puwedeng nagdesisyon na magbawas ng timbang, magkaroon ng mas mara­ming oras para sa pamilya, o mag-ipon ng mas marami pang pera. Maaaring ang ibang goals ay may tendency na mabigo dahil hindi magagawa ang mga to-do-list sa kabisihan, pero huwag payagan na maapektuhan ang pag-save ng pera.

Ang healthy cushion sa pag-iipon ay makatutulong na harapin ang mga hindi inaasahang financial na problema.

Ang pag-iipon ng pera ay nagsisimula sa simpleng konsepto na bawasan ang gastos. Mas konti ang paggasta mas makakaipon ‘di ba.

>BAKIT MAHALAGA MAG IPON

Mahalaga ang mag-ipon dahil may mga bagay hindi inaasahang pangyayari na maaari nating maranasan. Halimbawa, ikaw o ang isa sa miyembro ng iyong pamilya ay nagkasakit, magagamit mo ang iyong naipon upang maipambili ng mga pangangailangan para sa agarang paggaling. Isa pang halimbawa ay ang pandemyang ating nararanasan, kung may ipon ka sa ganitong sitwasyon ay hindi ka mahihirapan sa mga pangunahing pangangailangan. Hindi sa lahat ng oras ay palagi tayong may pera, ang pag-iipon ay isang matalinong paghahanda para sa mas maayos ng bukas.


30. the difficulties of speaking in english faced challenges the grade 11 12gas studenten casian senior high school sy 2021 2022 on academic performance​


There are several challenges that Grade 11 and 12 GAS students may face when speaking in English on their academic performance. These may include:

1. Lack of confidence in speaking English: Due to the lack of practice and proficiency in the language, students may find it difficult to communicate confidently in English. As a result, they may be less likely to take part in class discussions and group activities, which can lead to lower academic performance.

2. Difficulty understanding and expressing complex concepts: English is a complex language and students may find it difficult to express complex ideas and concepts in English. This can make it difficult to understand and articulate the material being taught in class.

3. Difficulty with pronunciation and grammar: English has a wide range of pronunciation and grammar rules, which can be difficult to master. This can make it difficult for students to communicate effectively with their peers and teachers.

These challenges can be overcome by providing students with additional support and resources to help them master the language. This can include providing online resources, extra classes and tutoring to help students improve their English language skills. Additionally, teachers should be patient and encouraging when helping students improve their English language skills.
