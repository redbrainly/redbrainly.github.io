18. C Tayug Montessori anticipates additional expenses of P15,400.00 for a new equipment needed foroffering Senior High School 2 years from now How much should be invested in an account that eams10% compounded monthly?​


PRT:

=======================

PER YEAR

First, converting R percent to r a decimal

r = R/100 = 10%/100 = 0.1 per year,

then, solving our equation

I = 14500 × 0.1 × 2 = 2900

I = $ 2,900.00

The simple interest accumulated

on a principal of $ 14,500.00

at a rate of 10% per year

for 2 years is $ 2,900.00.

[tex]\sf 2,900.00[/tex]

PRT:

===========================

PER MONTHLY

First, converting R percent to r a decimal

r = R/100 = 10%/100 = 0.1 per year,

putting time into years for simplicity,

1 months ÷ 12 months/year = 0.083333 years,

then, solving our equation

I = 14500 × 0.1 × 0.083333 = 120.83285

I = $ 120.83

The simple interest accumulated

on a principal of $ 14,500.00

at a rate of 10% per year

for 0.083333 years (1 months) is $ 120.83.

[tex]\sf 12.83[/tex]
