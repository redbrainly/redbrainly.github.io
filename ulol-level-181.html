28. what is greater 181 lbs or 54 kg​


Answer: 181 lbs is greater.

Step-by-step explanation:

To compare the numbers properly, we first need to convert the units. In this case, we will be converting kg to lbs. (1 kg = 2.20 lbs)

54 kg x 2.20 lbs = 118.8 or 119 lbs

181 lbs > 119 lbs (54 kg)

Therefore, 181 lbs is greater.
