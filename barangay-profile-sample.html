3. could you suggest a profile to be include in your barangay development plan that address the challenges of covid​


Answer:

Health and Wellness: This profile will focus on implementing measures to prevent the spread of COVID-19 in the barangay, such as providing information and education on proper hygiene practices, setting up quarantine facilities for individuals who have tested positive for COVID-19, and ensuring that there are enough healthcare workers to attend to the needs of the community.Economic Recovery: This profile will focus on addressing the economic impact of COVID-19 on the barangay, such as providing financial assistance to small businesses and micro-entrepreneurs, implementing programs to encourage local commerce, and working with the local government to create job opportunities.Community Support: This profile will focus on providing support to the most vulnerable members of the community, such as the elderly, people with disabilities, and low-income families. This can include providing food assistance, establishing a community response team to provide assistance to those in need, and creating programs to promote mental health and well-being.Infrastructure Development: This profile will focus on ensuring that the barangay has the necessary infrastructure to support the community during the pandemic, such as setting up isolation centers, providing clean water and sanitation facilities, and ensuring that there is reliable internet access for online learning and telemedicine.Education: This profile will focus on ensuring that children in the barangay have access to education, despite the challenges posed by the pandemic. This can include providing online learning resources, establishing alternative learning modalities, and working with the local government to provide education assistance to families.

Answer:

1. Establish a Social Support Network: Create an online platform for residents to connect with each other, share resources and stay connected during this challenging time.

2. Develop Digital Infrastructure: Invest in digital infrastructure to ensure that residents have access to reliable internet connection and can engage in remote work, e-commerce, and other digital activities.

3. Strengthen Public Health Services: Enhance public health services in the barangay to ensure that all residents have access to quality health care and preventive services.

4. Expand Access to Social Services: Provide social services to vulnerable populations, such as elderly, disabled, and homeless individuals, to ensure that their basic needs are met.

5. Enhance Financial Assistance: Establish a financial assistance program to help local businesses and families affected by the pandemic.

6. Establish a Resiliency Plan: Create a comprehensive plan to ensure that the barangay is prepared to respond to any future crises.

7. Raise Awareness: Launch an awareness campaign to educate residents about the importance of following safety protocols and maintaining social distancing.

VICENTE LUGAGAY: He Received P300,000 In Prizes As 2018 Gawad Saka Winner In Fish Culture

Agri News

zbsarian January 14, 2019

VICENTE LUGAGAY HOLDING A TILAPIA BREEDER

A 53-year-old former ordinary farm worker in a fish farm quit his job and put up his own tilapia hatchery in Brgy. Rizal, Santiago City in Isabela. In 2018, he was the Gawad Saka winner in the Fish Culture Category and received P300,000 in prizes from the national and regional levels. Of course, that’s peanuts compared to his income from the sales of his tilapia fingerlings.

Vicente Lugagay produces 300,000 to 500,000 fingerlings a month which he sells not only in Isabela but also in other places in Nueva Vizcaya, Cagayan, Pampanga and the Cordillera Administrave Region.

Lugagay won his national and regional awards because of the practical and innovative technologies he has adopted in running his tilapia hatchery

CONTRIBUTIONS:

MORE STURDY FINGERLINGS – Lugagay also installed a special pump in his conditioning tanks instead of the conventional flow-through and aeration system. This allows for more sturdy fingerlings, thereby higher survival. Sex reversal is achieved in only four days instead of the usual 21 days.

CONTRIBUTIONS:

The farm size is 150 hectares, with 40 hectares devoted to aquaculture. We grow tilapia along with redclaw crayfish and Malayan prawn in the same ponds. We also grow vegetables and raise sheep on the rest of the acreage.

29. a method that involes the use of DNA profiling done thru blood tests or tissue samples of parents & child to determine whether the child is a biological offspring of the parents.A. Paternity test.C.maternity test B.DNA testD.blood test​


Answer:

A and C Paternity and Maternity Test

Explanation:

DNA paternity testing is the use of DNA profiles to determine whether an individual is the biological parent of another individual. Likewise, a maternity test is used for an individual to find their biological mother. Both are correct in this case since the question did not specify the sex of the parent. But in case you are only allowed to choose one, answer A. A paternity test is more common than a maternity test.
