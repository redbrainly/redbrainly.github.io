9. arithmetic mean between 5 and 35 soluton


ARITHMETIC MEAN

Arithmetic mean between 5 and 35.

SOLUTION:

Just add the two arithmetic extremes and divide it by 2.

[tex]5 + 35 = 40 \\ 40 \div 2 = 20[/tex]

Therefore, the common difference is obviously 15 and the missing arithmetic mean is 20.
