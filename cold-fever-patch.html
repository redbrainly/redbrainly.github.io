11. If you have a fever, should you be in cold place or hot place?Example: Aircone blowing cold air or warm air?​


It is generally recommended to be in a cool place when you have a fever, as this can help bring down your body temperature and make you more comfortable. A fever is a sign that your body is fighting off an infection or another type of illness, and a high body temperature is a natural response to this. While being in a warm place may feel more comfortable at first, it can actually make your fever worse by further increasing your body temperature.

If you have a fever, you should try to stay in a room that is cool, but not too cold. You can use a fan or an air conditioner to help regulate the temperature and make you more comfortable. Avoid bundling up in blankets or clothing, as this can cause your body temperature to rise even further. Instead, wear lightweight, loose-fitting clothing to help regulate your body temperature and keep you comfortable.

It is also important to stay hydrated when you have a fever, as a fever can cause you to lose fluids more quickly. Drinking plenty of fluids, such as water and electrolyte-rich beverages, can help prevent dehydration and make you feel better.

Overall, it is generally best to be in a cool place when you have a fever, as this can help bring down your body temperature and make you more comfortable. However, it is important to listen to your body and do what feels best for you. If you are feeling too cold, you can adjust the temperature or add a light blanket to stay warm.
