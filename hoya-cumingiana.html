14. Direction: Write the following ratios in its lowest terms. Ratio Lowest term 5-6) What is the ratio of 9 hoya plants to 81 butterflies? 7-8) What is the ratio of 25 mangoes to 100 rubber trees? 9-10) What is the ratio of 12 moonlight philodendrons to 48 stars?​


Answer:

5-6) 1:9

7-8) 1:4

9-10) 1:4

Step-by-step explanation:

5-6)

The ratio of hoya plants to butterflies is 9:81. To get the lowest term, divide both numbers by 9:

9 ÷ 9 = 1

81 ÷ 9 = 9

Thus, 1:9

7-8) The ratio of mangoes to rubber trees is 25:100. To find the lowest term, divide both numbers by 25:

25 ÷ 25 = 1

100 ÷ 25 = 4

Thus, 1:4

9-10) The ratio of moonlight philodendrons to stars is 12:48. To get the lowest term, divide both numbers by 12:

12 ÷ 12 = 1

48 ÷ 12 = 4

Thus, 1:4
