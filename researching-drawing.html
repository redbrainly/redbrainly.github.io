9. 23. The first step in the research process begins with generating research ideas for research problemsand synthesis.a. Establishing research problems and synthesisb. Formulating research designs and methodologyC. Collecting datad. Drawing conclusions and recommendations​


Answer:

The Research Process is the investigation and study of problems in a systematic way. The final conclusion is based on reliable sources and established facts.

Explanation:

These are the Basic Steps in the Research Process or Problems:

1. Establishing Research Problems and Synthesis.

This is the first part of any research problem it needs to Identify the problem or data that needs to get a conclusion. Also creating a synthesis will help you lead the possible solutions and it gives a path on where to look for the resources.

2. Preliminary search of Information

checks whether there is enough source of information available to come up with final conclusions.

3. Locate Materials

Initialize locating the possible source of information such as Books, Keywords, Authors, geographical location, etc.

4. Evaluate the Sources

It needs to be credible, truthful, and reliable.

5. Make or Take down Notes

As the resources are available, gather the information from it and start taking down important information.

6. Prepare the draft

Organize the collected information and start creating drafts for the conclusion.

7. Proofread and write the conclusions

The final step in writing the conclusions is based on the sources of information.

14. why do researchers draw samples instead of examining the entire population?​


Answer:

Usually, a sample of the population is used in research, as it is easier and cost-effective to process a smaller subset of the population rather than the entire group. The measurable characteristic of the population like the mean or standard deviation is known as the parameter.
