Hala Bira is an Aklanon language meaning “dispense all means”

Explanation:

Hala Bira Iloilo! An Aklanon term that is shouted during the Ati-Atihan dance of the Dinagyang Festival, which means to “dispense of all things, in order to share what you have”.

Answer:

ibuhos at ibigay ang lahat

Explanation:

Ang Hala Bira ay isang wikang aklanon na nangangahulugang "ibuhos / ibigay ang lahat ng paraan".
