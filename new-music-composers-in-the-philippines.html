20. 1.What are the key concepts/ideas of contemporary Philippine music (Traditional to New music?2. How do you describe Kundiman?3. Who are the famous Filipino kundiman composers?​


Answer:

1.What are the key concepts/ideas of contemporary Philippine music (Traditional to New music?)

Traditional music

⇔ The traditional musics of the Philippines are usually used by woodwind brass, and percussion instruments, and have fewer lyrics, as the tones, dynamics, and melodies are more important than lyrics for their contemporary style. Composers in the Philippines once believed that the tone of the song speaks more than the words of the song or the lyrics of it.

New music

⇔ The new music in the Philippines keeps up with the latest trends and adapts music styles that are popular in the twentieth century until now in the industry of entertainment. Other new musics in the Philippines are usually influenced by the music of the people in the west.
