3. What is the name of the wrap around skirt that the women of the Southern Kalinga wore? A. Kain, B. Palda, C. Sayal, D. Tapis


Answer:

D. tapis

While many motifs and colours are shared across the region, Kalinga clothing is particularly brightly coloured and is often dominated by bands of fiery red. This type of wraparound skirt is generally known as tapis in the Tagalog language and as kain to the Kalinga.
