Health Profesionals: Anna Layug, Cindy Puno, BHW

Barangay San Sebastian

Together with the local Barangay Officers and the Health workers, the barangay introduced Information Dissemination on Fight Against Covid 19. A mobile speaker round the barangay every weekends to inform and remind each household to stay at home and do the necessary means to avoid acquiring the virus. They remind everyone to always wear  face masks and face shields and continue washing hands as often as they need. Barangay officials also handed out free boxes of face masks and infographics against Covid 19.

The officers also informed the people that once there is a family member who is already showing signs of the virus they should coordinate with the barangay so that preventive measures can be done immediately and avoid the upsurge of the virus.

In case a member of the barangay tested positive for the virus the household would be locked down and the officers would unconditionally provide support to the members of the household. #BrainlyFast
