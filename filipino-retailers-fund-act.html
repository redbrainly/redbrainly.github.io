Explanation:

Administrative Order No. 142, s. 1955

Signed on September 23, 1955

MALACAÑANG

MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 142

DESIGNATING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL BANK AS THE ADMINISTRATOR OF THE FILIPINO RETAILERS’ FUND, WITH THE PROVINCIAL, CITY, AND MUNICIPAL TREASURERS AS ITS DEPUTIES.

27. sino nag tatag Filipino Retailers Fund Act ( National Marketing Corporation"​


Answer:

The Retailers Fund act implemented by Carlos P. Garcia is something that gave encouragement & also support to Philippine native retail stores. The Government price stabilization agencies guided those Filipino retailers for their merchandizing activities improvement & to partcipate in distribution, for them to stabilize the prices/amounts of commodities in short supply.
