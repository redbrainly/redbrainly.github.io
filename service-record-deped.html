14. What agency tracks the activities of a typhoon that enters PARS?

D. Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical, and Astronomical Services Administration (PAGASA)

English:

PAGASA monitors tropical cyclone activity and issues warnings if they fall within the Philippine Area of Responsibility (PAR).

Tagalog:

Sinusubaybayan ng PAGASA ang aktibidad ng tropical cyclone at nagbibigay ng mga babala kung ito ay nasa loob ng Philippine Area of Responsibility (PAR).
