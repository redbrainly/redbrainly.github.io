13. sample detailed lesson plan in elementary level​


Answer:

Subject: English

Grade level: 4th grade

Duration: 45 minutes

Objectives:To introduce the concept of nounsTo identify common and proper nounsTo practice writing sentences using proper nouns

Materials:Whiteboard and markersChart paper and markersHandouts (worksheet on nouns)

Introduction (10 minutes):Greet the students and ask them how their day is going.Write the word "nouns" on the whiteboard and ask the students if they know what it means.Explain to the students that nouns are words that describe people, places, things, or ideas.Write a few examples on the whiteboard (e.g. dog, city, love, book, etc.).Ask the students if they can guess which ones are proper nouns.

Direct Instruction (20 minutes):Explain the difference between common nouns and proper nouns.Write "Common Nouns" and "Proper Nouns" on the chart paper and place it on the board.Write examples of common nouns (e.g. cat, street, happiness, etc.) under "Common Nouns".Write examples of proper nouns (e.g. Siamese, Main Street, Joy, etc.) under "Proper Nouns".Explain that proper nouns always start with a capital letter and refer to a specific person, place, or thing.Write a few sentences on the board and ask the students to identify the proper nouns in each sentence.

Guided Practice (10 minutes):Distribute the handouts to the students and explain that they are going to practice writing sentences using proper nouns.Give the students 5-7 minutes to complete the worksheet.Walk around the classroom to monitor the students and offer help if needed.

Closure (5 minutes):Call on a few students to share their sentences and highlight the proper nouns in each sentence.Summarize what the students learned during the lesson.Remind the students that proper nouns are important to use in writing to make their sentences clear and specific.Assessment:Observation of students during the guided practice activityCompleted handoutsTeacher-made quiz on proper nouns next lesson.
