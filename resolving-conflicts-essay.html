5. how to write an argumentative essay about resolving conflict


Develop your thesis.

-What is the purpose of the essay and its main idea?

 

Write introduction.

-Give the main concept to be discussed

-Incorporate your essay question in the introduction

 

Formulate an outline.

-This will include all the arguments you might have.

 

Write the body or content.

-Use the STEW structure

S - Statement - main idea of the paragraph

T - Techique - state the techniques using verbs of doing like suggests, reveals, creates

E - Example - Include a text from your source

W - Why - this should answer why the technique is effective, why has this technique been used, and why does this evidence support your opinion and answer your essay question.
