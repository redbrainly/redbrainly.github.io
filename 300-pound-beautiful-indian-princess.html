The indian princess or La belle Sauvage, is a musical play with a libretto by James Nelson Barker and music by John Bray , based on the pocahontas story as originally recorded in John Smith’s The General Historie of Virginia ( 1624 ) . The piece is structured in the style of a Ballad-opera , with songs and choruses , and also has music underlying dialogue , like a melodrama . Pocahontas persuades her father , king Powhatan , to free smith and becomes attracted to John Rolfe , breaking off her arranged marriage with a neightboring tribal prince , an action that leads to war . Her tribe wins the war , but her father loses trust in the white settlers; Pocahontas warns the settlers who reconcile with powhatan . Several comic romances end happily , and smith predicts a great future for the new country.
