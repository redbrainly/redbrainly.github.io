3. 8. What is the most significant thing in life to succeed: persistence or hard work?​


Explanation:

preposition that will complete

It is difficult to say which one is the most significant as both persistence and hard work are important in achieving success. Persistence is the ability to keep going despite facing obstacles and setbacks, it involves the determination to not give up and to keep trying until one reaches their goals. On the other hand, hard work is the effort put into achieving a goal, it involves dedicating time, energy and resources towards achieving a desired outcome.

Both persistence and hard work are essential in achieving success as they complement each other. Persistence provides the drive and determination to keep going, while hard work provides the necessary effort and resources to make progress. Without persistence, hard work may not be sustained over time and without hard work, persistence may not bring about any results.
