25. In Sean's fish tank, there are 6 times as many goldfish as guppies. There are a total of 21 fish in the tank. How many more goldfish are there than guppies


Let:
x = guppies
y = goldfish

guppies = goldfish
x = 6y

A group of fish is 7 units, one guppy and six goldfish.

21 ÷ 7 = 3

There are three groups of fish, so: 3*(x + 6y)
21 = 3x + 18y

Three guppies and eighteen goldfish.

18 - 3 = 15
There are 15 more goldfish then guppies.
