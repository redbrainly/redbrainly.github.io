1. What do you think was the purpose of the board display?​


Display board

Display Board is a learning tool in the form of a board or hanger which is now often used by teachers to display the work or student learning outcomes in the form of products. This display board is actually a container or place to provide important information that can support learning in the classroom.

The information in question can be in the form of student portfolios, concepts or material being studied, for example on the board there are a series of pictures showing the process of metamorphosis in frogs, it can also be in the form of themes or slogans that form the basis of a teaching and learning activity to be carried out.

Display board purpose

The purpose of displaying student work or more commonly referred to as this display are:

As a place to explain events, school rules, lists and other information and encourage student interest in school work.

As a place to stick various types of work or student work.

As a form of appreciation for the efforts that have been made by students in completing a task or job

Increase student motivation, because after all the quality of the work produced will get the same opportunity to be displayed

Provide information, both general in nature, such as posters or slogans, as well as those related to learning, such as charts/charts/graphs, work steps/formulas, etc.

As a decoration that can beautify the classroom atmosphere.

As an evaluation material for teachers and students as well as parents through displays/displays listed on the display board.

Because often the display board is also considered a semi-portfolio, the display also aims to display the results of class wealth
