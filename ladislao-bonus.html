Answer:

600,000 -income before deductions for the bonus and income taxes

600,000 ÷ 24 months

= 25,000 -monthly income from 2015-2016

25,000 × 5 ÷ 100

= 1,250 -bonus monthly

1,250 × 24

= 30,000 -total bonus for 2015 and 2016

600,000 + 30,000

=630,000 -income before deduction of the bonus

30,000 × 30 ÷ 100

= 9,000 -tax deduction for the income bonus

630,000 × 30 ÷ 100

= 189,000 -tax deduction for the over all income with bonus from 2015-2016

630,000 - 189,000

= 441,000 -Final income from 2015-2016
