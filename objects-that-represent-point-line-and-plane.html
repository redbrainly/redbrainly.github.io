25. give examples of objects that represent as point,line,and plane


Points, Lines, and Planes:

Examples of objects that represent points, lines, and planes are the following:

Points:tip of a pen corner of the room finger tip door knob tip of the needle Lines: a ruler a curtain rod sides of a rectangular mirror lines of the pad paper stripes of the shirt Planes: the blackboard top of the table walls of the room front side of the door cover of the book

Definitions:

A point is the most basic building block of Geometry. It is represented by a dot and is named after the big letter of the alphabet.

Examples:

tip of a pencil corner of a box tip of a pin

A line is a straight, continuous arrangement of infinitely numerous points. It has infinite length but has no thickness. It extends in opposite directions.

Examples:

lines of the notebook ruler sides of the bond paper

A plane is a geometric figure that has both length and width but has no thickness. Often contains both the point and the line.

Examples:

side walls of the cabinet top of the box back cover of the book
