4. In your health class, you were tasked to create a poster about global health, among all issues of global health, you choose cancer in children and adolescents. How will you make your output informative and creative ?


Answer:

1. Research and gather information: Make sure you have enough information about the topic. This will include the causes, symptoms, and treatments of cancer in children and adolescents, as well as statistics and trends related to the issue.

2. Choose a visually appealing design: Use eye-catching colors, graphics, and images that are related to the topic. Make sure the design is not cluttered and is easy to read.

3. Present the information in an organized and concise manner: Use headings, bullet points, and charts to present the information in an easy-to-understand format. Avoid using technical jargon, and make sure the information is accessible to all audiences.

4. Highlight the impact of cancer in children and adolescents: Emphasize the impact of the disease on the affected children and their families, as well as the impact on society as a whole.

5. Include a call to action: Encourage viewers to take action by promoting awareness and support for research, treatment, and care for children and adolescents with cancer.
