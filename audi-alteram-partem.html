Explanation:

Commonalities

The Ilocos and Cordillera region are known to have unique textiles.

Difference

the Ilocanos prefer to have an intricate geometric design, weavers from the Cordillera province takes on a different design. These weavers would oftentimes include designs that speak of their way of life having a family of farmers.

Difference

Cordillera textile is much simpler. Women would have clothes that are usually of red, green and blue colors at most.Textile from the Ilocos Region celebrates the weaving traditions of Abel fabrics.
