29. This refers to an instance where in boys build up a warm and loving relationship with mothers (mommy's boy)..pa help​


Many benefits are felt when parents and children have a close emotional relationship. Some of them are children who have stable emotions, cultivate intelligence, and have high self-confidence. This is why it is very important to build closeness in the family, especially between the son and his mother.

In the period of young adults, parents, especially mothers, have new duties and roles in line with the changing needs of children at this time. The changes that occur during this period are physical, cognitive, and social. Children will begin to break away from dependence on their family and begin to focus on social life outside the home. The challenge for parents is how to strike a balance between maintaining family ties and increasing the child's autonomy as the child ages and matures.

In one study, it was stated that parents who maintain good and warm communication have children with better outcomes in social life, do not use drugs, and experience fewer anxiety disorders and depression than children with parents who do not maintain communication in their teens and young adults.

The success of children's growth and development is influenced by environmental factors, ranging from the family environment to the wider community. Especially for boys, if the relationship with the mother is close and full of love, then the child can adapt to the outside environment, even though it will be difficult to be influenced by bad things.
