8. Rio and Kim had the same amount of money at the start. When Rio gave Kim 300 pesos to Kim. The ratio of Rio's money to Kim's is 2:3. How much money did they have at the start?


Original amount of money:

Rio: x
Kim: x

New amount of money with the ratio of 2:3.

Rio: x - 300   (gave 300 to Kim)
Kim: x + 300 (add 300 the amount from Rio)

Solution:

(x - 300) : (x + 300) = 2:3

Equate the product of means to the product of extremes:

2 (x+300) = 3 (x - 300)
2x + 600 = 3x - 900
3x - 2x = 600 + 900
x = 1,500

ANSWER: The original amount of money each has is 1,500 pesos.

Check:

Rio's money to Kim's money = 2:3

(1,500-300) / (1,500 + 300) = 2/3
1,200 / 1,800 = 2/3

Divide the numerator 1,200 and denominator 1,800 by their GCF 600 to simplify to the lowest term:

(1,200/600) / (1,800/600) = 2/3
2/3 = 2/3  (True)

28. kong wong yai instrument​


Answer:

Gong

Explanation:

The Khong Wong Yai is called Gong in English but different in number to tune the sounds, beeswax with not exactly amount is pasted under the bossed. It is a Thai's percussion instrument usually involves some kind of striking on nipple gong. It is composed of 16 units of Gong as a circle of a rattan frame.

Answer:

The khong wong yai is a circle with gongs used in the music of Thailand. It has 16 tuned bossed gongs in a rattan frame and is played with two beaters.The player sits in the center of the circle. It is used in the piphat ensemble to provide the skeletal melody the other instruments of the elaborate ensemble.
