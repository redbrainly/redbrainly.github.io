1. my cousins went to Mararison Island but they didn't swim simple sentence or compound sentence ​


Compound sentence

Explanation:

The sentence 'My cousins went to Mararison Island, but they didn't swim' is an example of a compound sentence.

A compound sentence is made up of two independent clauses. Using the example above, there are two independent clauses which is

- My cousins went to Mararison Island

- but they didn't swim

A compound sentence also has a coordinating conjunction ( for, and, nor, but, or, yet, so) and a comma(,) or by a semicolon (;) alone.
