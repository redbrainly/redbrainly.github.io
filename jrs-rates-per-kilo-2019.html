Step-by-step explanation:

400,000 as initial or first term.

20 as the 20th term.

5% or 0.05 as the rate increase

we need to get first the common difference

400,000 × 0.05 = 20,000

20,000 is the common difference

Arithmetic sequence will be used.

a[sub]20 = a[sub1] + (20 - 1)20,000

= 400,000 + 19(20,000)

= 400,000 + 380,000

a[sub]20 = 780,000

4. 38 3/5 * t RJ's car travels km per hour. JR's car is faster than RJ's car by 3 4/5 km per hour. How fast is JR's car?​


Answer:

42 km/hr

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's call the speed of RJ's car "s". So, s = 38 3/5 km/hr.

The speed of JR's car is s + 3 4/5 km/hr = 38 3/5 + 3 4/5 km/hr = 42 km/hr.

So, JR's car is traveling at a speed of 42 km/hr.
