13. Anu-ano ang mga epekto ng La Niña? (English) What is the effects of La Niña? Please answer in English or Filipino,Thanks!


Explanation:

La Niña is often associated with wet conditions in some parts of Asia including the Philippines. It will bring more rains, slightly cooler temperatures, and moderate to strong tropical cyclone activities.

La Niña is a phenomenon that describes cooler than normal ocean surface temperatures in the Eastern and Central Pacific Ocean, regions close to the equator off the west coast of South America.

La Niña means "little girl child" in Latin. This is described as the extended periods of heavy rains and storms. This consequences of this are the following:

Flooding. Continuous rains for days can fill dams and thus water needs to be released. This can flood the low-lying areas near the dam. The same applies to cities with poor drainage systems.

Landslides. Continuous rains can soften the slopes, causing it to erode and eventually destroy a portion of the mountain.

Health Concerns. Flooding can cause health alerts for diseases like leptospirosis due to poor drainage system where rats can reside. The bacteria that causes leptospirosis comes from the urine of rats.
