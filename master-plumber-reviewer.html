9. If the daily wage of cpm and a plumber are in the ratio 2:1. in a day, a cpm has to work 8 hrs but the plumber only 6 hrs. determine the ratio of their hour wages.


Answer:

Sure thing :)

Explanation:

If the daily wage of a CPM and a plumber are in the ratio 2:1, it means that the CPM earns twice as much as the plumber.

Let's assume that the plumber earns x amount of money per day.

If the CPM earns twice as much, then the CPM earns 2x per day.

We know that a CPM works 8 hours a day and a plumber works 6 hours a day.

So, we can find the hourly wage of each worker by dividing their daily wage by the number of hours they work.

For the plumber: x/6 = plumber's hourly wage

For the CPM: 2x/8 = CPM's hourly wage

We can see that the CPM's hourly wage is (2/8) times the plumber's hourly wage, which is the same as (1/4) times.

So the ratio of their hourly wages is 1:4, meaning the CPM earns 4 times more per hour than the plumber.

Hour: x
