4. effect on addiction of online games​


Answer:

online game addiction can have severe negative consequences. Though most of the symptoms listed above have short-term effects, they can lead to more severe long-term repercussions if not addressed properly. For example, someone addicted to video games will often avoid sleeping or eating proper meals in order to continue gaming. While the short-term effects of this may include hunger and fatigue, it could eventually lead to a sleep disorder or diet-related health issues. Similarly, those who isolate themselves from others in order to play video games may miss out on family events, outings with friends, or other events in the short-term. 25. speech for gaming addiction


Answer:

Good morning. Today I’d like to talk to you about the growing issue of gaming addiction.

Recent studies have shown that gaming addiction has become increasingly prevalent in young people. With the rise of online gaming, it is easier than ever for individuals to get completely consumed by their virtual worlds. This ultimately leads to an unhealthy addiction, which can have serious consequences on an individual’s physical, mental and social health.

So what can be done to combat this dangerous trend? Firstly, parents need to be aware of the signs of gaming addiction. These can include changes in sleep patterns, social isolation, changes in appetite, and mood swings. If you notice any of these signs in your children, it’s important to take action to help them.

Secondly, it’s important to set boundaries and limits when it comes to gaming. Make sure to set a designated amount of time that your children can spend playing games, and make sure that they stick to it.

Finally, it’s important to encourage other activities outside of gaming. Encourage your children to participate in other hobbies and activities, such as sports, music, or art. This will help to keep them engaged and also help to prevent them from becoming too consumed in their virtual world.

In conclusion, gaming addiction is a growing issue that needs to be addressed. As parents, it’s important to be aware of the signs, set boundaries, and encourage other activities in order to prevent our children from becoming addicted. Thank you.

Dear friends and fellow gamers,

We all know the joy and excitement that gaming brings to our lives. It provides a sense of accomplishment, a sense of community, and a sense of escape from the stresses and challenges of the world. But for some of us, gaming can also become a source of pain and suffering.

Gaming addiction is a real and growing problem. It can lead to a loss of control, a loss of relationships, and a loss of hope. It can make us feel alone, even when we are surrounded by people who care about us. And it can make us feel helpless, even when we have the power to change our own lives.

But there is hope. We can overcome gaming addiction, and we can do it together. By reaching out for help, by supporting each other, and by taking back control of our lives, we can break the cycle of addiction and find a new path to happiness and fulfillment.

So let us come together, as a community, and commit to facing this challenge head on. Let us support each other, let us seek help when we need it, and let us never give up hope. Together, we can overcome gaming addiction and build a brighter future for ourselves and for each other.

Thank you.
