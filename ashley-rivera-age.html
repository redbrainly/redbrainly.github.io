10. Solve the following using working backward. Draw your answer inside the box. Do this in your notebook. Ashley, Anna, and Aileen are sisters. Ashley is half younger than Anna Anna is 2 years older than Alleen. Anna and Aileen's combined age is equal to 14 years. How old is Ashley?​


Problem:

Ashley, Anna, and Aileen are sisters. Ashley is half younger than Anna. Anna is 2 years older than Aileen. Anna and Aileen's combined age is equal to 14 years. How old is Ashley?

Solution:

Ashley, Anna, and Aileen are sisters. Ashley is half younger than Anna. Anna is 2 years older than Aileen

Let x/2 be the Ashley's age Then let 2 + x be the Anna's ageAnd let x be the Aileen's age

Anna and Aileen's combined age is equal to 14 years

(Aileen's age) + (Anna's age) = 14x + (2 + x) = 14

Solve the value of x which is the Aileen's age to the problem equations.

(Aileen's age) + (Anna's age) = 14x + (2 + x) = 142x + 2 = 142x = 14 - 22x = 122x/2 = 12/2x = 6

Substitute the Anna's age age to the present age which is the value of x to the solving the problem equation.

Anna's age = 2 + xAnna's age = 2 + (6)Anna's age = 8

Substitute the Ashley's age to the present age which is the value of Anna's age.

Ashley's age = x/2Ashley's age = (8)/2Ashley's age = 4

Answer:

∴ Therefore, the present age of Ashley is 4 years old, Anna is 8 years old and Aileen is 6 years old.
