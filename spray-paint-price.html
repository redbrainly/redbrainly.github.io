16. explain how Boyle's law is demonstrated in the spray paint can ?​


Answer:

As Boyle's Law states that with a decrease in the pressure of a gas, the volume of gas increases. So, when the nozzle of spray paint is pressed, with a sudden decrease in the pressure of a gas, the volume of pressurized gas increases. Therefore, highly compressed gas expands drastically.

18. how to clean spray paint equipment​


Answer:

Clean filter, guard and SwitchTip in flushing fluid (water, paint thinner etc.). Remove siphon tube set from paint and place in flushing fluid. Use water for water base paint and mineral spirits for oil base paint. If sprayer has a filter, unscrew bowl, remove filter assembly.

19. How is electric charge used in paint spraying? ​


A negative charge is applied to the paint while it's in the reservoir. A charge is applied in the barrel of the spray paint gun. As the paint is propelled through the gun, it rubs against the side of the nozzle and gains a negative electric charge.
