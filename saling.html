N - Net Sales

D - Sales Discounts

R - Sales Return

G - Gross Sale

Let us given them their values.

N - P450,000

D - P35,000

R - P15,000

To find gross, we need to add them all.

G = N+D+R

G = P450,000 + P35,000 + P15,000
