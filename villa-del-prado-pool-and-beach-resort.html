9. The lifeguard of the beach resort is on his post which is 4 m high. A boy in the pool is 9 meters away from him. Find the angle of depression from the lifeguard to the boy.​


ANGLE OF DEPRESSION

===========================================

[tex]\large \sf \underline{Problem:}[/tex]

The lifeguard of the beach resort is on his post which is 4 m high. A boy in the pool is 9 meters away from him. Find the angle of depression from the lifeguard to the boy.

===========================================

[tex] \large \sf \underline{Answer:}[/tex]

[tex]\qquad \qquad\qquad\qquad \huge \rm 24°[/tex]

===========================================

[tex] \large \sf \underline{Solution:}[/tex]

[tex]\large \tt{ tan \: x \: = \: \frac{opposite}{adjacent} }[/tex]

[tex]\large \tt{tan \: x \: = \: \frac{4}{9}}[/tex]

[tex]\large \tt{ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: x \: = \: tan ^{ - 1} \: (0.444)}[/tex]

[tex]\large \tt {\: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: x \: = \: 23.96 ° }[/tex]

[tex]\:\:\:\:\:\:\:\: \large \boxed{\tt{\red{ x \: = \: 24° }}}[/tex]

Therefore the angle of depression from the lifeguard to the boy is 24°.

One of the top ten first-class resorts in the Philippines is Antulang Beach Resort. The hotel features its own underwater coral structures that support a marine eco-system.
