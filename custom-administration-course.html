16. What is the relevance of ICT in your chosen course? ​(public administration)


Answer:

iCT or Information and Communications Technology is essential in the course of studying Public Administration.

What is Public Administration?

Public administration is a field in which leaders serve communities for the common good. It is also concerned about the implementation of government policies all for the common good.

Public Administration as a Course

A public administration degree prepares graduates to work in nonprofit organizations, government agencies, and other social and civil service institutions. The curriculum provides students with an overview of the field, combining survey courses in areas such as public service and project management with more specific studies in areas like grant writing.

What is Expected from A Public Administration Graduate?

A public administration graduate must be familiar with the laws of the government and must know the process of legislating laws. He/she must know the process of drafting laws. He/she must know how to provide truthful information. He/she must know how to plan, organize, direct and direct governmental operations similar to the function of business administrators, but should be within the context of the public and government offices.

RELEVANCE OF ICT TO PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION

Knowledge in ICT aids public administrators in the drafting or legislation of laws. Legislation requires thorough research, document drafting and processing. Thanks to ICT and these are all made easier.

ICT also plays a very important role in disseminating truthful information which is what public administrators do. Filtering of truthful information through research and spreading it through print media and broadcast media has been easier with ICT.

Part of a public administrator's job as well is to document events that may be used for the common good. ICT has made the documentation job easier with the presence of smart phones and computers.

Currently, most government processes have been automated and these were all made possible with ICT. Processes and transactions have been made easier.

As a conclusion, ICT is making every part of a public administrator's work easier. Knowledge in ICT will help every graduate of public administration prepare for their jobs in the real world which makes it relevant to the course itself.

What is meant by public administration?

Public administration is a social science that studies how to manage a public organization in the context of state life and the institutions within it. In this field, it is closely related to various regulations and public policies, state objectives, development administration, and ethics that regulate state administration.

What are the studies in public administration?

• Governance

• Policy implementation by the government

• Bureaucratic behavior

• Human Resource Management

What is the purpose of public administration?

Public administration has the main goal of trying to achieve a state goal in accordance with the wishes of the public or society. which includes security, welfare, and social justice.

In an effort to achieve these public goals, of course, there is a need for support, control, social participation, and a sense of responsibility from the implementers of public administration in carrying out their duties.
