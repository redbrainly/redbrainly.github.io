27. Calculate the potential of a copper electrode immersed in


The potential of a copper electrode immersed in 0.0440 M Cu (NO₃)₂ = 0.2968 V

Further explanation  

Cell potential (E °) is the potential difference between the two electrodes in an electrochemical cell.  

The value of E cells uses a reference electrode which is used as a comparison called the Standard Electrode which is the hydrogen-platinum electrode  

The cell potential for nonstandard conditions we can use the Nernst equation  

[tex]\rm E=E^o-\dfrac{RT}{nF} lnQ[/tex]

For standard temperature T = 298 K,  

[tex]\rm E=E^o-\dfrac{0.0592V}{n} log\:Q[/tex]

Q = the reaction quotient  

E° = standart cell potential

R = gas constant (8.314JK⁻¹mol⁻¹

n = number of moles of electron tansferred in balance equation

F = Faraday constant =  96485 C mol⁻¹

Because the task is incomplete, we assume the electrode is  immersed in a solution of 0.0440 M Cu (NO₃)₂

Then , Reaction of half cell :

2e- + Cu²⁺ (aq)⇒ Cu(s) (2 electron transferred)

E° reduction for Cu = +0.337 V

For Q  = ratio of product concentration to reactants, and because the solid and liquid forms are not counted, then :

[tex]\rm Q=\dfrac{1}{[Cu^{2+}]}[/tex]

then assuming in a standard state, the equation becomes

[tex]\rm E=E^o-\dfrac{0.0592V}{n} log\:Q\\\\E=0.337-\dfrac{0.0592}{2}log\dfrac{1}{[[Cu^{2+}]}\\\\E=0.337-\dfrac{0.0592}{2}log\dfrac{1}{0.044}\\\\E=0.337-0.0402=\boxed{\bold{0.2968~V }}[/tex]

Understand communication style

It is important for you to understand the communication style of the people in your work environment, but sometimes this is not easy. So it takes time to understand someone's communication style. Basically there are 4 communication styles that you should know, which are as follows:

Passive

People with this style usually look ignorant of something. They usually often fail to express feelings or needs.

Aggressive

It's easy enough to see the intent of someone who is communicating aggressively. However, they often issue orders, ask questions harshly and fail to listen to others.

Passive-Aggressive

The passive-aggressive communication style is quite difficult to understand. Because usually people with this communication style appear passive on the surface, but inside they are very active.

Assertive

People with this communication style are criteria for co-workers that you can work well with. Communicating with them will be easier. Because the assertive communication style can express their ideas and feelings well, without sidestepping the interests of others.
