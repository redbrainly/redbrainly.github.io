The final round in Mathematics Quiz Bee during the Mathematics Week Celebration consists of 5 questions, The marking scheme is as follows: 5 points for every correct answer; -2 points for every incorrect answer; and 0 point for every question not answered.a. What is the minimum score for the quiz bee?b. What is the maximum score for the quiz bee?c. Write a situation where a contestant scores 5 points in the quiz bee.d. Write a situation where a contestant scores -1 for the quize. If you are one of the contestants, what the tihings you should bear in mind in order to earn the most possible score​


Answer:

a. The minimum score for the quiz bee is -10 points because 5 x (-2) = -10.

b. The maximum score for the quiz bee is 25 points because 5 x 5 = 25.

c. A situation where a contestant scores 5 points in the quiz bee is if they answered 1 question correctly and did not answer or answered the other 4 questions incorrectly.

d. A situation where a contestant scores -1 for the quiz is if they answered 1 question incorrectly and did not answer or answered the other 4 questions correctly.

e. Some things a contestant should bear in mind in order to earn the most possible score are: studying and practicing beforehand, carefully reading and understanding each question, and taking the time to think through each answer before choosing the most correct one.

21. Elsa joined as Science and Mathematics quiz bee on television there are 30 questions.18 questions are about Math. what is the percentage of science questions?​


Answer:

My answer is "40%"

Explanation:

Because 18 is 60% of 30 so the remaining percent is 40%.

Answer:

40 %

Step-by-step explanation:

1. Subtract the number of Math questions to the total questions.

30 - 18 = 12

2. Divide the difference by the number of questions and multiply the quotient by 100.

12 / 30 = .4

.4 x 100 = 40

Lastly, move two decimal places to the left to make it a percent number.

.40 — 40%

If 40% is the percentage of Science questions, then 18 questions is equal to 60% and is also the percentage for Math questions.

23. Write a script about presenting a subject quiz bee in a meeting


1. The contest is open to all students-who are enrolled in GECC or General Education Core Curriculum under Arts and Sciences.

2. During the contest proper the following materials will be used A board (provided by the facilitator) > Writing material (chalk)

> Eraser (tissue) 3. The questions will be from General Information, Social Science, Natural Science,

Language and Mathematics.

4. The contest will have 3 rounds: Easy, Average, and Difficult round. There will be ten (10) questions for the EASY, five (5) questions for the AVERAGE, and five (5)

questions for the DIFFICULT round. 5. The quizmaster shall read each question twice. After the second reading, the quizmaster shall say the word "GO". Only then the contestants shall begin writing their answers on the boards provided. The time allotment is 10 seconds for regular questions

and 20 seconds for math questions. 6. After the time limit and buzzer sound, the contestant will automatically stop writing The contestants should raise their answer and the correct answer will be flashed on the

screen.

7. Any protest or appeals regarding the preceding question will be entertained before the

quizmaster proceed to the next question.
