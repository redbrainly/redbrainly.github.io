Sa katunayan, ang paggawa ng isang monologue upang pagyamanin ang isang drama script ay hindi kasing dali ng iniisip mo. Ang isang mahusay na monologue ay dapat na maglabas ng mga detalye ng balangkas at mga character nang hindi sinisira ang buong drama o pinunit ang kamatayan ng madla; bilang karagdagan, ang isang de kalidad na monologue ay dapat ding makapagpahayag ng mga saloobin ng isang tauhan at mag-ambag sa pagdaragdag ng damdamin at pag-igting sa buong natitirang drama. Interesado sa paggawa ng isang monologue? Talaga, ang iyong monologue ay maaaring magamit upang magdagdag ng detalye sa isang character o upang madagdagan ang tindi ng drama sa kabuuan. 14. pahelp pong gumawa ng script "about COVID-19"​


Answer:

Clinician: Hello is this Ms. McNally? I’m Dr. Back from the COVID-19 response team.

Family: Hi.

Clinician: I understand your father has tested positive for COVID-19.

Family: Yes. I’m very worried. I feel guilty that he got it in a nursing home. I thought that place would be ok.

Clinician: Anyone would be worried. And there is no way you could have known this would happen.

Family: I suppose so.

Clinician: Is it ok if we talk about what COVID-19 means for your father?

Family: Please.

Clinician: May I first ask if you are the person who makes medical decisions for him.

Family: Yes. I’m his surrogate whatever you call it. I have the papers.

Clinician: Perfect. I like to make sure I’m talking to the right person.

Family: That’s me.

Clinician: I need to give you some background. Most people who get COVID-19 have a mild or moderate illness and don’t need the hospital. The people who most often get severe pneumonia with COVID are older and have existing medical problems, like your father.

Family: You know, I’ve heard that on the news but have been afraid to think about it.

Clinician: Well that’s a very normal reaction. COVID has put all of us in a tough situation.

Family: Yes. And I know that you are so busy.

Clinician: We are doing our best under the circumstances. So I hope your father has a mild case and can stay where he is. We can make sure he gets all the treatments he needs. However, if his COVID becomes severe, it will almost certainly take his life.

Family: I was afraid you would say that.

Clinician: It’s not what any family member wants to hear. Given that, if the worst-case scenario happened and he was going to die, do you think he would rather be in the hospital or be at home—I mean at his nursing home.

Family: Gosh. That’s a big decision.

Clinician: I can see that you want the best for him.

Family: Absolutely.

Clinician: Let me put it another way. If he didn’t have dementia and was as sharp as you or me. And he understood COVID-19 and what would happen if he got a severe case. If he were sitting here with us, what would he say?

Family: Oh he would say, enough already. I’ll stay here. But I don’t know.

Clinician: It sounds to me like if you put on his hat, and become him, he’d say "enough already." But if you put on your hat, you’d say, "I don’t know." Do I have that right?

Family: Yes. I’m not ready to lose him.

Clinician: Tell me more.

Family: He’s always been there for me and my kids. He’s the backbone of the family. He always believed in me.

Clinician: Would he believe in you now? To speak for him?

Family: When you put it like that, I know the answer. I just don’t like it.

Clinician: It’s not the kind of decision anyone wants to make. It does sound like you two may have talked about this?

Family: He told me when he was first diagnosed with dementia, back then he was just a little forgetful, nothing big. We were driving to the park to walk the dog. He turned to me and said, "Remember, when I can’t do this anymore, it’s time to let me go."

Clinician: Wow, thank you for telling me that.

Family: I had kind of forgotten about that. It's funny—I can see him saying it to me.

Clinician: Hmm. That kind of memory is a gift. Would it be ok to honor that?

Family: Now it’s clear to me. Let’s keep at him at his home.

Answer:

ok

Explanation:

Pangangalaga sa Kapaligiran

Ang pangangalaga sa kapaligiran ay isang napakahalagang isyu na kinakaharap ngayon ng ating mundo. Sa loob ng mga nakaraang dekada, maraming nakakabahalang mga epekto na ang naganap dahil sa hindi tamang pangangalaga sa kalikasan. Sa kasalukuyan, nakikita natin ang mga senyales ng climate change, pagkawala ng mga species ng hayop at halaman, pagkawala ng mga natural na kagandahan ng mundo, at iba pa. Upang malutas ang mga problemang ito, kailangan nating gawin ang lahat ng ating makakaya upang pangalagaan at protektahan ang kalikasan.

Ang pangangalaga sa kapaligiran ay hindi lamang isang tungkulin ng mga gobyerno at organisasyon. Ito ay isang tungkulin ng lahat ng tao. Kailangan nating maging responsable sa ating mga gawi at desisyon upang hindi mas lalong mapinsala ang kalikasan. May mga simpleng hakbang na maaari nating gawin upang mag-ambag sa pangangalaga sa kapaligiran. Halimbawa, pwede nating bawasan ang ating paggamit ng plastik at styrofoam, dahil ito ay matagal bago ito ma-decompose. Sa halip, pwede tayong gumamit ng reusable na mga gamit tulad ng mga reusable na bag, tumbler, at container. Pwede rin tayong magtanim ng mga halaman sa ating mga bakuran, upang makatulong sa pagpapalinis ng hangin at sa pagpapalaganap ng mga natural na kagandahan.

Bukod pa sa mga simpleng hakbang na ito, may mga mas malalaking hakbang na pwede nating gawin upang mas pangalagaan ang kalikasan. Halimbawa, kailangan nating maging responsable sa paggamit ng mga hindi renewable na mga resources tulad ng langis, gas, at iba pa. Pwede nating gamitin ang mga renewable na mga resources tulad ng solar at wind power upang makatulong sa pagpapababa ng mga greenhouse gas emissions. Kailangan rin nating suportahan ang mga organisasyon at programa na naglalayong pangalagaan ang kalikasan, tulad ng pagtitiyak na hindi nasisira ang mga natural na tirahan ng mga hayop.

Bukod sa mga hakbang na ito, mahalaga rin na magkaroon tayo ng edukasyon at kamalayan tungkol sa kalikasan at ang kahalagahan ng pangangalaga nito. Dapat nating ipasa ang kaalaman at kamalayan na ito sa mga susunod na henerasyon upang matiyak na ang pangangalaga sa kalikasan ay magpapatuloy sa hinaharap.

Sa huli, ang pangangalaga sa kapaligiran ay isang tungkulin ng lahat ng tao. Kailangan nating magtulungan upang masiguro na ang mundo ay magiging isang mas ligtas, mas malinis, at mas maganda na lugar para sa ating lahat at para sa susunod na mga henerasy
