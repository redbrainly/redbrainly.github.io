11. identify the connective tissue represented in these drawings​


Answer:

Dense regular tissue Dense irregular tissueLoose connective tissueAdipose tissue

Explanation:

To classify them under the microscope, here are some descriptions:

Dense Regular tissue- It is a classification of a fibrous connective tissue which are characterized by its fiber. Fiber is its main matrix element. It is mainly composed of collagen fibers. They appear as compact fibers oriented in one direction unlike the dense irregular.Dense irregular tissue - the fibrils of the tissue are oriented in multiple directions.Loose connective tissue - this is the most common type of tissue. An example of this would be the areolar tissues. They are usually characterized by its sparse matrix and fibrils. Adipose tissue - they usually appear as empty spaces. They look empty due to the extraction of fats due to the processing of the material.
