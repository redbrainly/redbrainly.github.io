2. A truck is carrying apple juice, mango juice, and orange juice bottles in a ratio of 3 : 1 : 3. If there are 16 mango juice bottles, then how many juice bottles in total are there?


✒️RATIO

[tex]••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••[/tex]

[tex] \large\underline{\mathbb{PROBLEM}:} [/tex]

A truck is carrying apple juice, mango juice, and orange juice bottles in a ratio of 3 : 1 : 3. If there are 16 mango juice bottles, then how many juice bottles in total are there?

[tex]••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••[/tex]

[tex] \large\underline{\mathbb{ANSWER}:} [/tex]

[tex] \qquad \Large \: \rm{112\:juice\:bottles} [/tex]

[tex]••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••[/tex]

[tex] \large\underline{\mathbb{SOLUTION}:} [/tex]

» Since the mango juices is in part ratio of 1. Let x be multiplied to 1 equal to the number of mango juice bottles. Find x.

[tex] 1x = 16 [/tex]

[tex] x = 16 [/tex]

» The value of x is 16. Multiply it to each part of the ratio then add them to find the total number of bottles.

[tex] 3x \::\: 1x \::\: 3x [/tex]

[tex] 3(16) \::\: 1(16) \::\: 3(16) [/tex]

[tex] 48 \::\: 16 \::\: 48 [/tex]

[tex] 48 + 16 + 48 = 112 [/tex]

[tex] \therefore [/tex] There are 112 juice bottles in the truck.

14. uses of tapioca flour? atleast 1-2 paragraphs​


Answer:

Use of tapioca flour

Tapioca flour, or tapioca starch, is a popular, gluten-free flour made from the starch of cassava root ( 1 ). It's perhaps best known for the thick, chewy texture it lends to gluten-free baked goods but also works well as an allergy-friendly thickener for sauces, soups, puddings, and stews.


15. Does tapioca healthy?​


Answer:

Generally, tapioca isn't the healthiest gluten-free starch on the market. Made up almost entirely of carbs, it is considered fat and protein-free with only minimal amounts of fiber. In fact, tapioca contains less than 0.1 percent of the amounts of the essential nutrients you need.

Tapioca is high in carbs and calories, so it is not a traditionally healthful food. However, it can help a person meet the recommended daily allowance of several important nutrients. It can also be a tasty, nutritious food choice for people who need to gain weight.
