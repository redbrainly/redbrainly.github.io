9. lina is thrice as old as elena. the difference of there age is 22 how old is lina and elenashow the complete solution​


[tex]\large\overbrace{\underbrace{\text{$>>>$ Question: $<<<$}}}[/tex]

Lina is thrice as old as Elena. The difference of their age is 22. How old is Lina and Elena?

[tex]\large\overbrace{\underbrace{\text{$>>>$ Solution: $<<<$}}}[/tex]

Let,

L = age of Lina

E = age of Elena

[tex]\large\overbrace{\underbrace{\text{$>>>$ Conditions: $<<<$}}}[/tex]

Lina is thrice as old as Elena,

L = 3E

The difference of their age is 22,

L - E = 22

but L = 3E, so

L - E = 22

3E - E = 22

2E = 22

E = 22/2

E = 11

Solve for L,

L = 3E

L = 3(11)

L = 33

[tex]\large\overbrace{\underbrace{\text{$>>>$ Answer: $<<<$}}}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{\large{\text{Therefore, Lina is 33 years old and Elena is 11 years old.}}}[/tex]
