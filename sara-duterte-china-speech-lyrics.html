17. What are the important speech of duterte in sona 2018


The State of the Nation Address (SONA) is an annual address done by the President of the Philippines to inform the nation about what was carried out in the previous year and the possible plan ahead.

Last July 23, 2018 was the third SONA of the 16th President of the Philippines, Pres. Rodrigo Roa Duterte. His SONA lasted for around 50 minutes only ; the shortest SONA.

During this 50-minute SONA the president mentioned the following topics: Drug war, Corruption, Mindanao Promise, Marawi Seige, Bangsamoro Law, Tax Reform, OFWs, Contractualization, Coconut Farmers, Mining Issues, Telecommunication Companies, Federalism, Health, Disaster Management, Boracay Closure, Land Use Act, and Foreign Policy. He also mentioned his controversial friend Bong Go, who is said to run for senate in next elections.
