16. Michael drove his car for 150 minutes at a constant speed of 75 miles per hour and then for another 2 hours at a constant speed of 81 miles per hour. how far did michael drive


Answer:

Explanation:

First we need to get the distance driven by Michael in the first part of the problem

divide by 60 to get hours

time/60 = 150/60 = 2.5 hours

multiply by time and speed to get distance covered in the first part

75 x 2.5 = 187.5km

to get the other distance we need to multiply

speed x time = 81 x 2 = 162km

now that we have the distance covered for both, we just need to add to find out how far michael drove.

187.5+162 = 349.5km

michael has driven 349.5km
