2)Identify:

•ac

where,

Two diagonals of a rectangle are equal,

ac = DB

apply this property of rectangle to find the value of ac

Given:

•ac = 2x + 10

•db = 4x - 6

Solution:

ac = db

2x + 10 = 4x - 6

2x - 4x = -6 -10

-2x = -16

-2x/-2 = -16/-2

x = 8

Plugin the value of x in ac equation to find the value of ac

ac = 2x + 10

= 2(8) + 10

= 16 + 10

ac = 26

Check:

ac = db

2x + 10 = 4x - 6

2(8) + 10 = 4(8) - 6

16 + 10 = 32 - 6

26 = 26

This proves that the two diagonals of the rectangle are equal.
