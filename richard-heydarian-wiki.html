3. _____ allows people to make voice calls through the Internet. a. VoIP b. Online Chat c. Videoconferencing d. Wiki


Letter A

VoIP or Voice Over IP is the one that allows people to make voice calls through the internet. It is the transmission of voice and other contents like multimedia (videos and pictures) over IP or internet protocol networks. Some VoIP allows people to call others using the same technology (VoIP) while others allow people to call someone who is using a telephone number, whether it is mobile, long distance or even long distance number.

VoIP serves as a converter in the network. In order for the information to be transmitted, VoIP changes the data into digital data. The use of another device is needed to bring back the information to its standard form (e.i., voice which is an analog data).

Answer:

A wiki is a type of website that is collaboratively generated from multiple users. It is a knowledge base website that allows it users to do collaborative editing and updating of its content and structure, per topic. There is no single author for a Wiki page or website.

Explanation:

The term "wiki" came from a local Hawaiian phrase, "wiki wiki," which means "super fast."  

How does Wiki works

A wiki is usually run using a wiki software, otherwise known as the wiki engine. A wiki engine is a type of content management system in that the content is produced without any defined owner or leader, and wikis have little intrinsic structure, allowing structure to emerge according to the needs of the users.

Types of Wiki Engines Bug Tracking Systems Open Source Proprietary

Example of a Wiki website

One best example of a large wiki is the Wikipedia, a free encyclopedia in many languages that anyone can edit. Wikipedia is not a single wiki, as what people might assume, but rather a collection of thousands of wikis, with each one pertaining to a specific language. Wikipedia provides one wiki page for each topic.

WikiHow: a public wiki page on how to do anything SAP Community Network Catawiki: a wiki of catalogs for collectors Open Street Map: creates and provides free geographic data and mapping Wikitionary: a multi-language dictionary

10. richard miles talambuhay


Answer:

Si Richard Miles (ipinanganak 1969)  ay isang British mananalaysay at arkeologo, na pinakilala sa paglalahad ng dalawang pangunahing serye ng dokumentaryo ng kasaysayan: Ang Sinaunang Daigdig ng BBC2 (2010),  na ipinakita ang isang komprehensibong pangkalahatang-ideya ng kasaysayan ng klasikal at bukang-liwayway ng sibilisasyon. at BBC Four's Archaeology: isang Lihim na Kasaysayan (2013).

Ipinanganak si Miles sa Pembury, Kent. Nag-aral siya ng sinaunang kasaysayan at arkeolohiya sa Unibersidad ng Liverpool at umupo para sa isang PhD sa mga klasiko sa ilalim ni Propesor Peter Garnsey sa Jesus College, Cambridge. Siya ay isang propesor ng Roman history at arkeolohiya at pro-vice-chancellor ng negosyo at pakikipag-ugnay sa University of Sydney.  Siya ang dating pinuno ng School of Philosophical and Historical Inquiry, at isang dating director ng Arts Career Ready Program sa Sydney.  Pangunahin ang kanyang pananaliksik tungkol sa kasaysayan ng Punic at Late Roman at arkeolohiya.

Inutusan niya ang mga archaeological digs sa Carthage at Rome, at noong 2010 ay inilathala niya ang Carthage Kailangang Wasakin: Ang Pagtaas at Pagbagsak ng Isang Sinaunang Pamamaraan ng Mediterranean.

Nag-host din siya ng dalawang bahagi ng serye ng Channel Four Television Corporation na Carthage: The Roman Holocaust (2004), na nakatuon sa digmaan sa pagitan ng Carthage at Roma.

15. Online forums are also called _____ Select one: a. Wikis b. Chat rooms c. Message boards d. Shared Calendar


The answer is C. Message Boards. Online forums are also called C. Message Boards. Online forums are also called internet forums or message boards. These are sites where online discussions are being held through the act of posting messages. It is to be noted that online forums or message boards are different from chat rooms.

Online Forums or "Message Boards" Online forums are also called message boards or internet forums. Online forums are sites where online discussions happen by posting messages. Sometimes, messages that are posted still need to be approved by a moderator before they can be seen by other people. Online Forums vs. Chat Rooms

Online forums are different from chat rooms. Here is the difference between the two:

Online forums - Messages are usually longer than a single line of text. Posts sometimes need to be approved by a moderator. Chat rooms - Messages in chat rooms are usually short, with a few number of words only. There is no approval needed for a message to be sent in chat rooms.

Online forums is another type of communication platform when using the internet.
