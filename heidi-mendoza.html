2. bagay na minamahal ni heidi mendoza


Sino si Heidi Mendoza?

Si Heidi Mendoza o mayroong kabuuang pangalan bilang Heidi Macaraan Lloce Mendoza ay isang dating taga-puna ng Commission on Audit sa bansang Pilipilnas. Isang kontrobersyal na maituturing ang kanyang panahon dahil sa kanyang pagsisiwalat sa mga katiwaliang nagaganap sa pamahalaan ng Pilipinas. Subalit sa kabila ng mga kontrobersyal ay matapang na hinarap ni Heidi ang lahat ng ito.  

Ang bagay na lubos na minahal ni Heidi Mendoza ay ang kanyang tungkulin na naglilingkod sa kanyang bayan sa pamamagitan ng pagawa ng mga kabutihan habang nasa posisyon sa gobyerno.

17. Taon kung kailan nalathala ang nobelang Heidi ni Johanna Spyri​


Answer:

1881

Explanation:

Heidi (/ˈhaɪdi/; German: [ˈhaɪdi]) is a work of children's fiction published in 1881 by Swiss author Johanna Spyri, originally published in two parts as Heidi: Her Years of Wandering and Learning[1] (German: Heidis Lehr- und Wanderjahre) and Heidi: How She Used What She Learned[2] (German: Heidi kann brauchen, was es gelernt hat).[3] It is a novel about the events in the life of a 5-year-old girl in her paternal grandfather's care in the Swiss Alps. It was written as a book "for children and those who love children" (as quoted from its subtitle).

22. What is the moral lesson of the story 'HEIDI'?


“Heidi” is a children’s story written by Johanna Spyri. It focuses on the life and adventures of an orphan girl named Heidi in the Swiss Alps and in Germany. There are several moral lessons that can be learned from the novel. Among them are the effective and restorative healing power of nature, as well as the inherent goodness and mercy of the Christian God.

30. what is the main idea of Heidi (other than the god moral)


Other than the God moral, the main idea of Heidi is that no matter where a person is, happiness can always be achieved.

For example:

- When Heidi is upset at first at moving to her grandfather's house, she eventually gets a good connection with him and they both love each other. When it comes to Heidi having to leave, they are both very upset.

- When Clara was living at her big house, but she was always given that stern treatment from the nanny (or whoever that lady was, I can't really remember). She went to Heidi's house for a holiday and her happiness bloomed, and she even learned how to walk.
