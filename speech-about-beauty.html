1. Informative speech 2 pharagraph about beauty or life or any topic


Answer:

Beauty is a highly subjective concept that can be difficult to define. However, most people would agree that it refers to qualities that are pleasing or attractive to the senses. Beauty is often associated with features such as symmetry, balance, and proportion, as well as qualities like kindness, compassion, and grace. In many cultures, beauty is also linked to cultural ideals and standards, which can vary widely.

One interesting aspect of beauty is that it is often closely tied to our emotions. When we see something that we find beautiful, we may feel a sense of joy or peace. This emotional response to beauty may be hardwired into our brains, as research has shown that the areas of the brain responsible for emotion and pleasure are activated when we experience beauty. Ultimately, beauty is a deeply personal and subjective experience, and what one person finds beautiful may be completely different from what another person finds attractive.
