Answer:

The following activities involve evaporation process:

-cooking noodles

-cooling after sweating

-boiling mongo seeds  

-salt making

-drying of sand after the rain

-drying of wet floor

-drying of plate after washing

-cooking rice​

These activities includes evaporation as by-process or last process. For example, in order for the floor to be air dried, the water in it should evaporate first. Evaporation is very common and one of the most processes happening in our daily lives. Even watering of plants involves the process of evaporation where the water that is absorbed by the plants' roots go the atmosphere on the form of vapor.
