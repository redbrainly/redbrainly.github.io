Severe Tropical Storm Brendan, also known in the Philippines as Tropical Storm Helming, was a third consecutive tropical cyclone to strike China in July 1991. A weak surface circulation developed near Carolina Islands in July 15. Tracking west-northwestward, the circulation organized into a tropical depression in July 19. The tropical depression intensified into Tropical Storm Brendan in July 21 when the depression approached Philippines. Brendan quickly intensified to reach the first peak intensity of 115 km/h (70 mph) when it made landfall in Luzon, Philippines.[nb 1] After emerging in Luzon Strait as a strong tropical storm and continued to track west-northwestward, Brendan reached the secondary peak intensity of 105 km/h (65 mph) in July 23, before making landfall on Guangdong, China, 30 kilometres (19 mi) southwest of Macau in July 24. After making landfall, Brendan weakened and dissipated later that day.

30. michael is 4 times as old as brandon and is also 27 years older than brandon


Answer:

Brandon is 9 years old.Michael is 36 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let us analyze the given age word problem.

What is asked?

Age of Brandon

Age of Michael

What are the given?

Michael's age - 4 times as old as Brandon

- 27 years older than Brandon

Solution:

In answering age word problem, we need to represent the missing value with a variable.

Let x be Brandon's age.

Then, for Michael:

4 times as old as Brandon - 4x

27 years older than Brandon - x + 27

To find Brandon's age, we are going to represent our algebraic equation as:

4x = x + 27

Let us solve. Combine similar terms.

Brandon's Age

4x = x + 27

4x - x = 27

3x = 27

3x/3 = 27/3

x = 9

We divided both sided by 3 to isolate x.

Brandon is 9 years old.

Michael's Age

4x

= 4(9)

= 36

Michael is 36 years old.

Checking:

Let us see if our answers satisfy the given condition.

Brandon - 9 ; Michael 36

Michael is 4 times as old as Brandon. ✔ It is correct because 36 is four 9s. (4 × 9 = 36)

Michael is 27 years older than Brandon. ✔ Yes, 36 is 27 more than 9. (36 - 9 = 27)

Final Answer:

Brandon is 9 years old.

Michael is 36 years old.

Age Word Problems

Age word problems are algebra problems that deal with the ages of people currently, in the past or in the future.

Age word problems are tricky so here are some of the important concepts and equations that can help in answering age problems.

If the present age is y, then n times the present age = ny.

If the present age is x, then age n years later/hence = x + n.

The ages in a ratio a: b will be ax and bx.

If the present age is x, then age n years ago = x – n.

If the current age is y, then 1/n of the age is y/n.

Let us define some terms related to age problems.

Algebraic Expression

This is how we express the given data in a problem. This is a combination of constant, variables and algebraic operations (+, -, ×, ÷).

Algebraic Equation

This is the equation made by the combination of algebraic expressions.
