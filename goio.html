3. Reflection for GOYO​


Answer:

The movie Goyo portrays a reality. Goyo is a teenager also and we cannot take that away from him. Yes, he is brave enough to be a leader, but just like us, as a part of being in the teenager stage, he becomes easily attracted to the things around him and become agressive.

Removing the word leader, Goyo is also a normal person just like us. He idolized someone also. But, the fun fact is that, Goyo really proves that if we idolized someone, sometimes, we dont realize that we became super focused on them already without thinking how about ourselves.

All I can say is that, everyone do mistakes but we should learn from it. Everyone must be very careful on their actions and be responsible enough as a Filipino citizen. Be brave enough and open our eyes and do not let our wants and pride eat us. Stand for what is right and fight.
