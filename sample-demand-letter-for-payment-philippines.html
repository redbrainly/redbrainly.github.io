5. johna purchased a condominium worth of 3,500,000,00 however the bank demands a 10% down payment how much the mortgage amount? Anu po sagot dto? ​


[tex]\large\underline{\rm{PROBLEM}:}[/tex]

Johna purchased a condominium worth of 3,500,000.00 however the bank demands a 10% down payment how much the mortgage amount?

[tex]\tt\color{red}{\overline{\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad}}[/tex]

[tex]\large\underline{\rm{ANSWER}:}[/tex]

[tex]\large\bold\color{red}{3,150,000}[/tex]

[tex]\tt\color{red}{\overline{\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad}}[/tex]

[tex]\large\underline{\rm{GIVEN}:}[/tex]

[tex]\small\bold{Down \: Payment \: Rate \: = \: 10\%, \: 0.1}[/tex]

[tex]\small\bold{Cash \: Price \: = \: 3,500,000.00}[/tex]

[tex]\tt\color{red}{\overline{\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad}}[/tex]

[tex]\large\underline{\rm{FORMULAS}:}[/tex]

➢ To find the mortgage amount here's the formulas that we could use.

[tex]\small\bold{Down \: Payment \: = \: Down \: Payment \: Rate \: × \: Cash \: Price}[/tex]

[tex]\small\bold{Mortgage \: = \: Down \: Payment \: - \: Cash \: Price}[/tex]

[tex]\tt\color{red}{\overline{\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad}}[/tex]

[tex]\large\underline{\rm{SOLUTION}:}[/tex]

➢ Since we have the down payment rate and cash price, we need to multiply and subtract it so we can get the answer.

[tex]\small\bold{M \: = \: (0.1)(3,500,000.00)}[/tex]

[tex]\small\bold{M \: = \: 3,500,000.00 \: - \: 350,000}[/tex]

[tex]\small\bold\color{olive}{M \: = \: 3,150,000}[/tex]

∴ Therefore, the mortgage amount will be 3,150,000.
