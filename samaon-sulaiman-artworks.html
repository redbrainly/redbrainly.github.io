1. who is samaon Sulaiman


Answer:

Samaon Sulaiman

Who is Samaon Sulaiman?

Samaon Sulaiman was a Filipino musician from Maguindanao known for his mastery of the indigenous kutyapi instrument. Samaon Sulaiman was conferred the title Manlilikha ng Bayan or National Living Treasure in 1993 for keeping alive traditional Maguindanao kutyapi music.

He was born on March 3, 1953 and died on May 21, 2011 at the age of 58. He learned to play the kutyapi from his uncle when he was about 13 years old. Aside from kutyapi, Samaon is also proficient in kulintang, agong, gandingan, palendag and tambul.
