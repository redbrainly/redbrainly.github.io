The religion of Islam has many similarities with the religion of Christianity, such as both religions believing in one God and the universal message of peace. One similarity between these two religions is their styles for marriage: Christian marriage and Moorish marriage.

Moorish marriages are similar to Christian marriages because they both occur within a religious setting; prayers, blessings, songs; however, Moorish marriages cannot be consummated until after the couple has obtained permission from a "representative" (usually a sheikh or imam).
