1. who is Sen Juan flavier? ​


Answer:

Juan Martin Flavier (Tagalog pronunciation: June 23, 1935 – October 30, 2014) was a Filipino physician and politician. He served as the Secretary of Health under President Fidel V. Ramos from 1992 to 1995, and was later elected to the Senate, serving from 1995 to 2007.
