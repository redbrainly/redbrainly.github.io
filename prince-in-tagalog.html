21. prince is john's father. prince is 45 years old. Five years ago, prince was twice as old as his son was then. how old is john now


Answer:[tex]\huge\blue{\overline{\qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \: \: }}[/tex]
Problem :

Prince is john's father. Prince is 45 years old. Five years ago, prince was twice as old as his son was then. how old is john now.


 »» 25 yrs old.


Solution :

To find the answer to this problem, carefully read to understand the problem that will help us know what to do to solve this problem.



Step 1:  Know what is given.

Prince's current age is 45 yrs old, five years ago Prince is twice ([tex]\times \bold 2[/tex]) as John's age.



Step 2: Subtract that 5 years to Prince's current age, then you will get the age of Prince 5 years ago. After you get Prince's age 5 years ago divide it to get John's age 5 years ago.

[tex]45 - 5 = 40[/tex][tex]40[/tex] ÷ [tex]2 = 20[/tex] (John's age 5 years ago)



Step 3: What is asked is John's age "now" so add that 5 years to John's age 5 years ago which is 20 to get John's current age.

[tex]20 + 5 = 25[/tex]


Therefore, John is 25 years old now.
