24. 2. What is the pH of a buffer made up of 0.5M NH, and 0.456M NH4CI? pKb NH₂ = 4.74​


[tex]NH_4 \leftrightharpoons NH_3^+ +H^+ \\\\\begin{matrix} & NH_4 & \leftrightharpoons & NH_3^+ & + & H^+ \\\text{initial} & 0.456 & & 0.5 & & 0 \\\text{change} & -x && x && x\\ \text{equilibrium} & 0.456-x &&0.5-x&&x\end{matrix}[/tex]

[tex]pK_b=-log{K_b} \\K_b=10^{-4.74} \\K_w=K_a\cdot K_b\\K_a=\frac{K_w}{K_b} = \frac{1\times 10^{-14}}{10^{-4.74}}=5.4954\times 10^{-10}[/tex]

[tex]K_a = \frac{[H^+][NH_3^+]}{[NH_4]}=\frac{(x)(0.5-x)}{0.456-x} = 5.4954\times 10^{-10}\\x=[H^+]=5.0118\times 10^{-10} M\\pH=-log[H^+]=9.3[/tex]

Using the Henderson-Hasselbalch equation:

[tex]pH=pK_a+log\frac{[A^-]}{[HA]}[/tex]

[tex]pH=(14-4.74)+log\frac{0.5}{0.456}=9.3[/tex]

- Globalization is not something happen in a zap it has a process to follow

- Drivers are the reasons why globalization takes place it can be people or events etc.

Globalization involves the collapsing of the borders

- Not necessarily borderlines between countries but breaking down figurative walls of ideologies that divide individuals among nations and government.

Globalization is inevitable

30. NH - Natural Hazard​


Answer:

natural hazard

Explanation:

A natural hazard is an extreme event that occurs naturally and causes harm to humans – or to other things that we care about, though usually the focus is on humans (which, we might note, is anthropocentric). An extreme event is simply an unusual event; it does not necessarily cause harm.
