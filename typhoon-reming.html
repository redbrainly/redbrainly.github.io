6. Natural calamity is one of the causes of proventy.for example,during typhoon Reming in legazpi City,having a population of 182,201,about 88% of it was displaced by the calamity.what percentage of the population was displaced by the typhoon?


Answer:

The problem states that the population of Legazpi City is 182,201 and that 88% of this population was displaced by the typhoon. To calculate the percentage of the population that was displaced, we can use the following formula:

Percentage displaced = (Number displaced / Total population) x 100%

Substituting the values given in the problem, we get:

Percentage displaced = (88 / 100) x 182,201 = 160,177.88

Rounding off to the nearest whole number, we get that about 160,178 people were displaced by the typhoon.

To calculate the percentage of the population that was displaced, we divide the number displaced by the total population and multiply by 100:

Percentage displaced = (160,178 / 182,201) x 100% = 87.9%

Therefore, about 87.9% of the population of Legazpi City was displaced by Typhoon Reming.
