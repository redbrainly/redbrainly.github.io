21. glenn received a paycheck of 18500 pesos. this amount reflects his monthly earnings after deducting 30% from his earning for taxes. how much was he paid before taxes were deducted? ​


Answer:

Glenn was paid 26,429 pesos before taxes.

Step-by-step explanation:

Net pay after taxes- the amount Glenn received after deducting taxes

Net pay after taxes: 18,500

Tax rate - income tax rate applied to gross pay

Tax rate: 30% or 0.30

Gross  pay - the total pay where tax rate is applied.

Gross pay: Unknown

Since 30% is is deducted from gross pay, the paycheck Glenn received is 70% of the gross pay before taxes.

Pay before taxes: x

(100% - 30%)x = 18,500

(70%)x = 18,500

0.7x = 18,500

0.7x/0.7 = 18,500/0.7

x = 26,428.57

x = 26,429

Glenn was paid 26,429 pesos before taxes.

To solve for the taxes paid at 30%:

Tax amount = (30%) × 26,429

Tax amount = (0.3) × 26,429

Tax amount = 7,928.70 or 7,929.00

Check:

Net Pay after taxes = Pay before taxes - Tax amount

Net Pay = 26,429 - 7,929

Net Pay = 18,500  (True)
