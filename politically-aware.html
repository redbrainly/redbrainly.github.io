9. Importance of being politically aware youth


Answer:

Being politically aware as a youth is essential for personal growth, empowerment, and active participation in building a better future for themselves and their communities. It empowers young people to have a voice and to be actively involved in shaping the society in which they live. It helps them develop critical thinking and analytical skills, research and evaluate information from various sources, form their own opinions and perspectives, engage in discussions and debates on political issues. It also enables them to make informed decisions when it comes to voting and participating in the democratic process, hope politicians accountable for their actions and decisions, and contribute to building a more just and equitable society. Overall, helps to foster a sense of responsibility and social awareness.

20. how can we improve our awareness on the social cultural and political changes​


Answer:

1. Get training for global citizenship

2. Bridge the culture gap with good communication skills

3. Practice good manners

4. Celebrate traditional holidays, festivals, and food

5. Observe and listen to foreign customers and colleagues

6. Pay attention to differences in culture

7.Be aware of different time zones

Explanation:

1. Get training for global citizenship

First, you should build the cultural knowledge of your employees.

2. Bridge the culture gap with good communication skills

Good communication skills are important when dealing with different cultures.

3. Practice good manners

Wherever you are in the world, it’s always good to have manners. In some countries, saying “please” and “thank you” is an important and crucial part of business conversation.

4. Celebrate traditional holidays, festivals, and food

Celebrating diversity can help increase cultural literacy, awareness, and acceptance.

5. Observe and listen to foreign customers and colleagues

Another way to help your employees become more culturally aware is to tell them to observe and listen to foreign customers and colleagues.

6. Pay attention to differences in culture

You can also help your employees be more culturally sensitive by telling them to pay attention to differences in a foreign customer’s culture.

7. Be aware of different time zones

When contacting foreign customers and meeting deadlines, tell your employees to be aware of the different time zones.
