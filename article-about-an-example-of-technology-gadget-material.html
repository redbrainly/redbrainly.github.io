19. useful of technological gadgets in traditional teenagers​


Answer:

Technology gadgets can be extremely beneficial for traditional teenagers in a multitude of ways. For instance, smartphones and laptops provide access to an endless wealth of information from the internet, which can help these teenagers expand their knowledge and understanding of the world around them. They can use these devices to access online resources, research papers, and even communicate with their teachers or fellow classmates.

Additionally, technological gadgets can help traditional teenagers to stay connected with their family and friends. Social media platforms and messaging apps enable them to stay in touch and share updates, which can be especially valuable for those who live far away from each other.

Furthermore, technology gadgets can provide traditional teenagers with a platform to express their creativity and hone their skills. They can use these devices to create digital art, music, and videos, or even to learn a new language or programming language.

Overall, technological gadgets have the potential to greatly benefit traditional teenagers by providing access to knowledge, facilitating communication, and fostering creativity. However, it's important for teenagers to use these devices responsibly and in moderation, as excessive use can have negative impacts on their mental and physical health.

24. 1. use technology/gadgets in the conduct of survey​


Answer:

What is a Technology Survey?

A technological survey is a method of gathering insights on software, tools, and relevant tech skills in a particular context. Technology surveys have a broad range—from being used to find out how tech-savvy your target audience is to conducting market research and collecting product feedback.

For instance, a school can conduct a technology survey to determine how much students know about the internet or how well students use the internet for learning. The data gathered through this process can help the students improve their knowledge as needed.
