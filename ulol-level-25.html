6. level1 cache is the smallest but the fastest among the different cache levels​. true or false


True

Cache is graded as Level 1 (L1), Level 2 (L2) and Level 3 (L3):

L1 is usually part of the CPU chip itself and is both the smallest and the fastest to access. Its size is often restricted to between 8 KB and 64 KB.

L2 and L3 caches are bigger than L1. They are extra caches built between the CPU and the RAM. Sometimes L2 is built into the CPU with L1. L2 and L3 caches take slightly longer to access than L1. The more L2 and L3 memory available, the faster a computer can run.

30. level of biological organization 1-25 pls can someone answer this ​


Answer:

1. Biological organization is the arrangement of different parts into progressively larger and more complex structures that work together to form a functioning whole.

2. At the lowest level, molecules are composed of atoms and interact with each other to form larger molecules.

3. Cells are the smallest units of life and are composed of molecules and organelles that carry out specific functions.

4. Tissues are composed of cells and are specialized for particular functions in the body.

5. Organs are composed of tissues and are specialized for specific functions in the body.

6. Organ systems are composed of organs and work together to carry out specific functions in the body.

7. An organism is composed of organ systems and is a living being capable of growth and reproduction.

8. Populations are composed of organisms of the same species living in the same area.

9. Communities are composed of populations of different species living together.

10. Ecosystems are composed of communities and their physical environment.

11. Biomes are composed of ecosystems that share the same climate and other environmental conditions.

12. The biosphere is composed of all the biomes on Earth.
