Adding subplots to create intertextual plots to the following plots,

1) Tom wakes up one day, finding himself stranded on a remote island.

Sub-plot: He tries to survive by creating an inanimate friend to keep his sanity until somebody comes to save him.

(Cast Away)

2) An old lady curses Jasmine to cut her eyelashes which would only grow to a meter again the next day.

Sub-plot: She runs into lots of problems and found herself on a path where nobody is alive goes there. She then learns that she is in a place where the old lady actually lives.

(Drag Me to Hell)

3) The entire barangay has been living in sin for the past ten years. Nick lives among them.

Sub-plot: One day, all of the people died of a natural disaster.

(The Story of Sodom and Gomorrah)

4) Sarah's mother dies in a plane crash. Her father grows into despair.

Sub-plot: Her father becomes a bad guy and tries to revive his wife.

(Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings)

5) Baby Tyler accidentally falls off the cliff while inside the car with his parents. The infant lands unharmed on a huge pile of leaves, a hundred meters below​.

Sub-plot: Tyler grows up and gets visions of what happened from when he was young. He then starts traveling back to the place where the accident happened and looks for clues whether it was really an accident or a crime.

(Tale of the Nine-Tailed)
