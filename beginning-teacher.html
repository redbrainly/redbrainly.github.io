13. Close encounter with a beginning professional teacher (1-3 years teaching experience)


Beginning in teaching journey is really difficult. Teaching is indeed a long learning process, flexibility is important and takes a lot of adjustments. May I share some of my activity in my few years of teaching. 
Preparing lessons and learning materials take a lot of time and effort. Thinking of a new way to discuss a lesson so students can easily understand. After all of the efforts still, more duties need to do checking examinations and others, computing grades and a lot of negotiation from parents, advisers and students.
Increasing responsibility and pressure from the government to improve curriculum and learning process. Also the responsibility towards every student monitoring and constantly guiding them.
Adapting to every student's attitude, ways of learning and supporting them in every school event. It takes a total effort to be a teacher.
No matter how long you teach, teaching is really difficult.

14. How would you use the Flanders system of instruction to provide feedback for a beginning teacher?​


Answer:

In this system, all teachers' statements are either indirect or direct. This classification gives central attention to the amount of freedom the teacher grants to the student. In a given situation therefore, a teacher has a choice. He can be direct, that is minimizing the freedom of the student to respond.
