15. In a school, for every 2 boys, there are 3 girls. What is the percent form of boys in the school? A. 70% B. 60% C. 50% D. 40%


Answer:

D. 40%

Step-by-step explanation:

If there are 5 students and 3 are girls and 2 are boys, then:

R=P/B*100%

R=2/5*100%

R=0.4*100%

R=40%
