Do you want to connect two 110V devices in series and power them from a 220V outlet, do you?

It’s possible, if the two devices consume the same power. (this is different from their power rating) Otherwise the lower power device would get higher voltage (and probably get damaged), the higher power one lower voltage than 110V. Devices with varying power consumption are not safe to operate this way, power fluctuations would result in probably dangerous voltage fluctuations. Basically you’re limited to two identical heating element or incandescent light bulb, to stay safe
