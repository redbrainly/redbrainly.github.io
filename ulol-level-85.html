13. 10. A submarine is 75 m below sea level. It rises 29 m and then dives 19 m. howfar is the submarine below sea level?a. 65 mb. 123 mc. 27 md. 85 ​


Let’s say sea level is zero. The submarine starts at -75. The first step is to add 29, to show the submarine rising. -75 + 29 = -46 The next step is to subtract 19, to show it diving. -46 - 19 = -65 Since -65 shows it being 65 below zero, or sea level, the answer is a. 65 m below sea level.
