1. A walkman uses four standards 1.5V batteries. How much resistance is in the circuit if it uses a current of 0.02A?​


Resistance is 75 Ω

Explanation:

Step 1: Enumerate the given values.

Given:

I = 0.02 A

V = 1.5 V

Step 2: Identify what is being asked.

Required:

Resistance

Step 3: Solve for Resistance

Equation:

The formula for Resistance is given by:

R = V/I

where: R - Resistance

I - Current

V - Voltage

Solution:

Substituting the value of V and I in the formula,

R = (1.5 V)/(0.02 A)

R = 75 Ω

Answer:

Problem:

A walkman uses a standard 1.5 V battery. How much resistance is in the circuit if it uses a current of 0.01 A?

Given:

V = 1.5V

I = 0.01A

Formula:

R = V ÷ I

Solution:

R = 1.5 ÷ 0.01

R = 1.49Ω

Answer:

1.49Ω
