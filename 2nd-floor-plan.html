20. Your friend falls down the stairs from the 2nd floor and can hardly stand. Immediate action to be taken?


Answer:

Explanation: Tell them to lie still. Assure them you are there-as well as getting them help. Call 911 or the emergency response team you have quick access to and give them the information they require from you. Continue to keep your friend as calm and comfortable as possible, but do NOT move them. Broken bones and things that are around those broken bones need to be kept totally immobile to reduce the chance of more injuries. The emergency responders should know how to best move your friend and transfer to a health care facility.

First Aid

Answer:

First aides are important especially if the injury can lead to a more serious matter that will definitely hinder the person’s function and productivity. Though, first aid is essential not all should attempt to do it especially those who don’t have any knowledge about it whatsoever. Since a person attempting to might cause more injury than what the patient has.

Examples of First Aid:

Putting a straight hard object to an assumed broken bone Applying disinfectant to a bruise or cut Wrapping of bandages Applying cold or hot compress

Simulation:

A friends fell down the stairs From about 1 floor He/ she can’t seems to stand

First Aid:

Do not force him or her to get up let him be in his position Ask him if he feel something off or painful If there is pain and its area is certain if possible move him to a more secure place Observe if there are bruises or swelling (might need compression) Call an adult for help/ call a hospital if the situation wasn’t neutralized
