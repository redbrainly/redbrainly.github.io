1. how do people find information that matches my needs?


People find information that matches their needs thru the use of searching tools online like search engines.  

Search engines are tools that are available in the internet wherein a user would type in any keyword pertaining to the information they are looking for. These keyword or keywords will be input in the search bar and in a matter of seconds, the search engine will return a list of websites related to the keyword given by the user. Priorities are set on the list. The first result will be the most relevant website and most related to the keyword provided.  

Search engine works thru the use of spiders or crawlers, which are special piece of codes that would crawls on the internet to gather as much website as he could. Examples of search engines are: Google, Yahoo, Bing.

Aside from search engines, there are websites that are called wiki, which contains articles contributed by people around the world. They are providing information to users as well. Example of wiki websites are Wikipedia, wiki sites.
