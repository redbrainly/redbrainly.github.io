1. difference between literature review and literature studies


Literature Review is a scholarly paper, which includes the current knowledge including substantive findings, as well as theoretical and methodological contributions to a particular topic, While Literature Studies is a study in which you read selected  text such as novels, short story or a poem and basically write a paper about it.


2. What the difference between literature and study?​


The Difference between Literature and Study

Literature and study can be distinguished in the following two example sentences. First, "the sun rises in the East sets in the West". This is a literature. Study would describe "Earth rotates on its axis". First, literature, figurative language, metaphor, and symbolism; there is no "sunrise from the East". That is the perception of people since long ago. The second is descriptive, fact-based language. Another example, the literary language, "man is a creature created by God who was expelled from heaven".

According to study "humans are the product of a long evolutionary process". So the language of study and literature is different. Literature expresses human wonder at the unknown, at the wonders and fears of nature. While study reveals the laws of nature. The equation, both science and literature work using language. Literary language is easy to understand and use by people in general, while the language of study is often only understood in a limited circle of the scientific community.

Literature is the result of human creativity that has a very high value. These values ​​are implied or expressed in a very beautiful and useful diction order for many people. However, this does not mean that simple fiction cannot be called literature. Precisely from this view, it becomes a necessity for authors to always hone their writing skills so that they will produce literary works that are getting better day by day towards the pinnacle (literary works that are beautiful and useful for many people).

Fiction works-poems, short stories, novels- are the result of word processing, imagination, and factual work by the author which, if trained continuously, will further improve the quality of his work. Writing fiction is becoming easier for more and more people to do with the existence of information technology as it is today.

It is different from study. The acquisition of study is carried out through a systematic process and steps in solving human and environmental problems.  In everyday life, study is still less popular in the community. The study seems to belong only to those on campus or laboratory spaces. Even simple problems can be approached with the study. To think and act scientifically one does not have to have an academic degree in study first.

Foreign Literature consists of foreign or from another country news, information websites, and articles that gives great relevance to your Research or Study. Foreign Studies are foreign researches, studies, thesis, surveys or any other methods of Research.

Foreign Literature gives a foreign background, that are relevant to the people and is shared from the people often refers to public news articles which also showed necessary data to help determine and support the Research of yours. In studies review, on the other hand, you analyze the works of other researchers (thesis, dissertation, etc.) mostly concentrating on their methodology, results, and conclusions.
