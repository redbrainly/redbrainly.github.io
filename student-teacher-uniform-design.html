13. What was the first filipino military uniform designs?


Until 1898, the few photos that are preserved show us officers wearing simple coats with a standing or rolled collar and pockets in the skirts, in white cloth or rayadillo. Also we have some models of rayadillo jackets with folds in the chest and the back and pockets in the skirts, inspired by the guayabera used by the Spaniards in Cuba but with a standing collar.

From 1898, however, the use of Norfolk jackets with vertical chest pockets in white cloth or rayadillo, in its different versions including folds and pockets in the skirts, will be popularized amongst officers. Illustrations: Museum of the Palace of Malacañang.

22. 15. There are 640 students in the auditorium. Of them, 20% are wearing PE uniform and the rest are wearing the school uniform. How many students are wearing school uniform? How many students are wearing PE uniform?​


Step-by-step explanation:

see the picture for the answer and solution:)

Answer:

Wearing PE uniforms: 128 students

Wearing school uniforms: 512 students

Step-by-step explanation:

To find out how many students are wearing PE uniforms:

First, convert 20% to a decimal.

20%= 0.2

Then multiply it by the number of students

(Number of Students) (0.2) = [Students wearing PE uniforms]

(640) (0.2)= 128

To find how many are wearing school uniforms:

Method one:

[Total number of students] - [number of students wearing PE uniforms] = [students wearing school uniforms]

Method two:

Knowing 100% minus 20% is 80%, convert 80% to a decimal.

80% = 0.8

Then multiply that by the total number of students

(Number of students) (0.8) = [students wearing school uniforms]

You get the same answer of 512, either way.
