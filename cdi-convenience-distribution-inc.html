3. What is the use of CDI in motorcycle​


Explanation:

CDI stands for Capacitor Discharge Ignition. The purpose is to provide electrical energy to the spark plug(s) at the correct time for ignition. This is a critical purpose. No or insufficient energy, or at the wrong time and your motorcycle is in the shop.
