step 1- The neck is the first step.

As you return your head to its previous position, take a deep breath.

Shoulders are the second step.

step 2-Arm circles, standing with your feet together, are a terrific technique to get circulation flowing to your shoulders before a workout.

Step 3 – Trunk Back Hips – A dynamic warmup stimulates blood flow and "activates" muscles that must be ready to function.

Step 4 – Trunk Twist – The key to this warm-up exercise is to just rotate your torso as far as it will allow you to.

Step 5 – Wrist- Rotate one elbow with a full hold on a barbell in the back or front rack position.
