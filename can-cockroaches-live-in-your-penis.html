Cockroaches and Bed Bugs are not the same insect. Bed bugs get their name because they typically harbour near their food source. (Human Blood) So mattresses, boxsprings, headboards, bed frames, and bed skirting are very common places to find them. Cockroaches also harbour close to a food source, and other than crumbs from that late night cookie, or your skin particles, the bed is not a good place for them to hang out. If you “german” cockroaches in your house, they will tend to be in kitchens and bathrooms.

Of course, there are always exceptions. I have seen bed bugs in every room just like cockroaches if the infestation is large enough.
