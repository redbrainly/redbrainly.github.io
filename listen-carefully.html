20. using a variety of strategies to listen carefully to others​


1. PAY ATTENTION

*Look at the speaker directly.

*Don't mentally prepare a rebuttal.

*Avoid being distracted my the environment factors.

*Put aside distracting thoughts.

*Listen to the speaker's boy language.

2. SHOW THAT YOU'RE LISTENING

*Nod occasionally.

*Smile and use other facial expression.

*Encourage the speaker to continue with a small verbal comments.

*Make sure that your posture is open and interested.

3. PROVIDE FEEDBACK

*Reflect what has been said paraphrasing.

*Ask question to clarify certain points.

*Summarize the speaker's comments periodically.

4. DEFER JUDGEMENT

*Allow the speaker to finish each point before asking question.

*Don't interrupt with counter arguments.

5. RESPOND APPROPRIATELY

*Assert your opinions respectfully.

*Treat other person in way that they want to be treated.
