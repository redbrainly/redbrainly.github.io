How to form a corporation

1. Hire a transactional attorney who can walk you through the business formation process

2. Appoint a registered agent and file the articles of incorporation

3. Create the corporate bylaws and appoint directors

4. Issue stock

5. File any other necessary documents with your local secretary of state

6. File any necessary IRS forms

What are the effects of the Corporate Food Regime?

The CORPORATE FOOD REGIME negatively affects society especially the very poor among us through their dependence on corporate manufactured food which lacks the needed nutrition. Farmers are also burdened by this through the passage of laws forcing them to buy patented seeds that are genetically modified by large corporations, failure to use these seeds would subject the poor farmers to face lawsuits by the company. And if you happen to grow these genetically identifiable seeds without permission, you can also be charged under the law, I mean, who really looks at seeds?  

The Corporate Food Regime is composed of institutions such as the World Trade Organization, the USDA, The World Bank, the Farm Bill, and others. These organizations have infiltrated and controlled many governmental bodies. Bribed politicians then create laws that force everyone to be dependent on corporate seeds and food.

This system which is spreading globally, really prevents small farmers from doing the natural thing which is just growing food without worrying about government intervention this, in turn, raises the food prices making it harder for the lower income people to buy natural food so they go for the corporate processed food alternative to feed themselves. Since these foods aren’t nourishing at all, they can expect to have health problems later in life.
