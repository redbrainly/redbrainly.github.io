Answer:

Key signature

1. Key of C

2. Key of G

3. Key of f

4. Key of G

Explanation:

Key signature is written at the beginning of a musical piece right after the clef. The key signature may be in the family of flat or sharp.

The first key in the sharp family that has one sharp is the Key of G.

The first key in the flat family that has one flat is the key of F.

1. Key of C because there is no sharp or flat.

2. Key of G because there is one sharp which is the F#.

3. Key of F because there is only one flat which is the Bb.

4. Key of G because there is one sharp which is the F#.
