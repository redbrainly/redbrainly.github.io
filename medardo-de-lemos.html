Answer:

64

Step-by-step explanation:

625=25²

So the side length of the farm is 25 m

The volume of each poultry house is 27 and the poultry houses is in the farm if a cube

So, 27=3x3x3=3³

So the length of each poultry house us 3m

25=3x8+1

8x8=64
