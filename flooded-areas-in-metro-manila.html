The Greater Manila Area is the contiguous urbanization surrounding the Metropolitan Manila area. This built-up zone includes Metro Manila and the neighboring provinces of Bulacan to the north, Cavite and Laguna to the south, and Rizal to the east.

Though sprawl continues to absorb new zones, some urban zones are independent clusters of settlements surrounded by non-urban areas.

In early 2021, during the COVID-19 pandemic, the area has also been referred to as the "NCR Plus", "NCR Plus Bubble" or "NCR+" (National Capital Region Plus) by authorities, with regards to the designation of community quarantines.
