How many possible permutations are there in a letters in the name “ARLENE”?

[tex]\underline{\mathbb{SOLUTION:}}[/tex]

» This 6-letter word can be arranged in 6! ways divided by 2! ways in which two E's are arranged.

[tex]\begin{aligned} & \bold{\color{lightblue}Formula:} \\ & \boxed{\: \rm P = \frac{n!}{n_1! \ n_2! \ ... \ n_k!} \:} \end{aligned}[/tex]

[tex]\begin{aligned} \rm P = \frac{6 \cdot 5 \cdot 4 \cdot 3 \cdot \cancel2}{ \cancel2} \end{aligned}[/tex]
