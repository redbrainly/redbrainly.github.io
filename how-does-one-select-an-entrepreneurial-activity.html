Entrepreneurial activities are those activities, the tasks and or functions of an entrepreneur. The person who is responsible for bringing all the factors of production together to create a good or service.

Commonly, in the process of selecting an entrepreneurial activity the context first needs to be understood.

For instance, are you saying what profession to become an entrepreneur in? Or what activities does an entrepreneur perform as an entrepreneur?

The first part can be answered by taking a look at your passion, where your interest lies and the few stakeholders that will be a part of your idea, business and team.

Must know the societies' needs and wants.When you already pick a certain need or want of societies - your target market -  you must know how to get to supply you and satisfy the demands of the people.What are the legal requirements you need to prepare?Do the location and nature of people living will be your demand?Who is your market and competitor?Describe your capital, future loss and return of profit.What will be your scope and limitation?What is your service/ product to be delivered?Who is your team?

A feasibility study will produce a business report and plan. It sometimes constitutes some business principles such as PDCA and other strategies. This will be the bible of an entrepreneur. What is a business plan? Read on https://brainly.ph/question/464327.
