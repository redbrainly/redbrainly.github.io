Step-by-step explanation:

W - T. Tirona Memorial National High School and Kalayaan Hospital.

To find the distance between two points, we should use the formula [tex]\rm distance = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}[/tex]

We are given that the point of T. Tirona Memorial National High School is (2,3), the point of Kalayaan Hospital is (-4, -12)

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{(-4 - 2)^2 + (-12 - 3)^2}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{(-6^2) + (-15)^2}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{36 + 225}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{261}[/tex]

Therefore √261 is W.

I. Island Cove and Binakayan Public Market

[tex]\rm distance = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}[/tex]

We are given that the point of Island Cove is (9,16), the point of Binakayan Public Market is (9,7).

[tex]\implies \rm distance =\sqrt{(9-9)^2 + (7 - 16)^2}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{(0)^2 + (-9)^2}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{81}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = 9[/tex]

Therefore 9 is I

N. St. Mary Magdalene Church and Municipal Hall.

[tex]\rm distance = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}[/tex]

We are given that the point of St. Mary Magdalene Church is (-2,5), the point of Municipal Hall (-6, -3)

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{(-6 - (-2))^2 + (-3 - 5)^2}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{(-4)^2 + (-8)^2}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{16 + 64}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{80}[/tex]

Since √80 is not given in the box, we will ignore N.

K. Aguinaldo Shrine and Binakayan Public Market

[tex]\rm distance = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}[/tex]

We are given that the point of Aguinaldo Shrine is (1,5), the point of Binakayan Public Market is (9,7)

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{(9 - 1)^2 + (7 - 5)^2}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{(7)^2 + (2)^2}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{68}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{4} \times \sqrt{17}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = 2\sqrt{17}[/tex]

Therefore 2√17 is K.

A. Save More and Fire Station

[tex]\rm distance = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}[/tex]

We are given that the point of Save More is (-3,3), the point of Fire Station is (-9.5,0)

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{(-9.5 - (-3))^2 - (0 - 3)^2}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{(-6.5)^2 + (-3)^2}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{42.25 + 9}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{51.25}[/tex]

Therefore √51.25 is A.

T. Water Camp and Island Cove

[tex]\rm distance = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}[/tex]

We are given that the point of Water Camp is (-5,1), the point of Island Cove is (9,16).

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{(9 - (-5))^2 + (16 - 1)^2}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{(14)^2 + (15)^2}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{196 + 225}[/tex]

[tex]\implies \rm distance = \sqrt{421}[/tex]

Therefore √421 is T.

25. pabor ba ang pogo sa pilipinas?


Nagpaliwanag ang Philippine Amusement and Gaming Corporation (PAGCOR) matapos umalma ang Chinese Embassy sa planong paglilipat sa hiwalay na lugar ng Chinese workers na nagta-trabaho sa ilalim ng Philippine Offshore Gaming Operations (POGO).

Sa isang panayam sinabi ni PAGCOR chair Andrea Domingo na may benepisyo pa ring matatanggap ang Chinese online gaming workers kahit pa ihiwalay ang mga ito mula sa mga Pilipinong manggagawa.

Katunayan, pabor pa umano para sa foreign workers ang kanilang hakbang dahil mas magkakaroon ng proteksyon ang mga ito sa lilipatang “self-contained communities” dahil kumpleto ito ng mga establisyemento.

Nilinaw din ni Domingo na hindi lilimitahan sa ibang lugar ang Chinese workers, gayundin na hindi haharangin ang kanilang karapatan bilang dayuhan.

Batay sa statement na inilabas ng Embahada, isinisi ng tanggapan sa Pilipinas kung bakit maraming illegal Chinese workers sa estado.

Philippine Offshore Gaming Operators (POGOs) is the official designation for firms operating in the Philippines which offer online gambling services to markets outside the Philippines.
